Kathiawar 6: Porbandar, Junagadh, Palitana

-- 1891110370 S From Mandavi Swamiji went to Porbandar. After about a week Akhandananda joined him. They stayed at the house of the Dewan Shankar Pandurang, with whom the Swami practised speaking in Sanskrit, and in that way increased his mastery of the language. (LSV 1.299)
-- 1891110380 CE From Porbandar Swamiji went to Junagadh again. Gangadhar Maharaj remained in Kathiawar, leaving Swamiji to move on alone. (LSV 1.299)
-- 1891110390 CE From Junagadh Swamiji went to Palitana - the last place in Kathiwar (LSV 1.300)
The Swami is next found at Palitana in Gujarat, where the holy mountain Shatrunjaya, sacred to the Jainas, is situated. Palitana is a city of temples, many of which date back to the eleventh century. High up on Shatrunjaya is a temple dedicated to Hanuman, and a Muslim shrine dedicated to Hengar, a Muslim saint. The Swami climbed to the top of the mountain to enjoy the view, which is magnificent. At Palitana he drew the attention of people because of his mastery of singing and playing on musical instruments. This was the last place in Kathiawar where he is known to have halted.
-- 1891110400 CE From Palitana Swamiji departed for Nadiad (LSV 1.300)
