Bhagalpur (July-Aug 1890)

1 Jul 1890 CE Swamiji sets out with Gangadhar mj for Himalayas. Did not return to the Math till Jan-1897 (LSV 1.243)
2 Aug 1890 CE Swamiji and Gangadhar Mj arrived first at Bhagalpur in the first week of August. (LSV 1.243)
3 Aug 1890 S Swamiji and Gangadhar Mj. Went and stayed for a week at the house of Babu Manmathanath. Met Mathuranath Sinha also. (LSV 1.243)
4 Aug 1890 S At a later date both of these gentlemen wrote their reminiscences of the Swami's visit at the request of one of Swamiji's disciples. (LSV 1.243)
5 Aug 1890 S Mathuranth Sinha writes his reminiscences of Swamiji's visit to Bhagalpur in his letter of August 11, 1905. (LSV 1.243)
6 Aug 1890 S Manmathababu, the Swami's host, was a staunch Brahmo, but became re-Hinduized by the Swami's eloquence and spiritual genius. The Swami explained to him the various aspects of Hindu religion and impressed him by his interpretations of the different episodes of Shri Krishna's life. (LSV 1.244)
7 Aug 1890 I In June 1906, Manmathababu wrote to a disciple of Swamiji. (LSV 1.244)
8 Aug 1890 I In June 1906, Manmathababu wrote to a disciple of Swamiji. (LSV 1.244)
9 Aug 1890 S In the same letter Manmathbabu writes about Swamiji's knowledge of Sanskrit (LSV 1.244)
10 Aug 1890 I In the same letter Manmathbabu writes about Swamiji's knowledge of music. (LSV 1.245)
11 Aug 1890 I In the same letter Manmathbabu writes about Swamiji's reluctance about meeting rich men of Bhagalpur (LSV 1.245)
12 Aug 1890 S In the same letter of June 1906, Manmathbabu recalls Swamiji's views on Sanyasi. He also likes swamiji's couple of new ideas. (LSV 1.245)
13 Aug 1890 CE In Bhagalpur Swamiji visited only two places while staying at Bhagalpur. The first occasion was when we went to see the holy man of Barari, the late Parvaticharan Mukhopadhya. (LSV 1.246)
14 Aug 1890 CE The second was when Swamiji paid a visit to the Temple of Nathanagar, one of the holy places of the Jaina community; and there Swamiji talked with the Jaina Acharyas on their religion. He was much pleased with his visits, and was also delighted to see the beauty of the scenery on the banks of the Ganga. He remarked, "These spots are very suitable for spiritual practices." (LSV 1.246)
15 Aug 1890 S Manmathbabu was pained to learn that some people criticized Swamiji for taking Sannyasa as he was not Brahmana. (LSV 1.246)
16 Aug 1890 S After about 7 days stay at Bhagalpur, Swamiji and Gangadhar Mj. Left when Manmathbabu was away. (LSV 1.246)
17 Aug 1890 CE Manmathbabu chased Swamiji up to Almora. Then gave up his idea. He could never bring swamiji back to Bhagalpur. (LSV 1.247)
18 Aug 1890 S Swamiji's stay at Bhagalpur left impression in mind of Manmathbabu (LSV 1.247)
