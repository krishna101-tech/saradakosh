New York: Vedanta for world - an Analysis (New Discovery)

1 Jan 1895 CE The focal point of Swamiji's interest during the early months of 1895 was in New York City, where he was embarking with all intensity upon a new and supremely important phase of his Western work. (ND 2.330)
2 Jan 1895 CE Swamiji had learned the need of the West for the philosophy of Vedanta, and he had seen how that philosophy could be applied to every problem of modern man. (ND 2.330)
3 Jan 1895 CE Swamiji's tour was fulfilling the function of a divine prophet to America-scattering the seeds of spirituality wherever he went and bestowing his blessing upon innumerable men and women. (ND 2.330)
4 Jan 1895 CE "Vivekananda is nothing if not a breaker of bondage." (ND 2.331)
5 Jan 1895 CE Swamiji did not come to America with a ready made and full-blown message, then the question that remains to be answered is how, why, and when it evolved? (ND 2.331)
