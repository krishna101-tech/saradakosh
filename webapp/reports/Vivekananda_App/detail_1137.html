Kutch, Bhuj

-- 1891110160 S Swamiji departed for Bhuj. He Stayed at Dewan's house. (LSV 1.293)
Soon after returning from Girnar to Junagadh, the Swami felt that he must be on the move. He therefore took leave of his friends, and with letters of introduction to officials at Bhuj, went on to that place. There he stayed with the Dewan.
-- 1891110170 S Swamiji had long discussions with Deawn, on the industrial, agricultural and other economic problems of the country, and on the need for the spread of education among the masses. (LSV 1.293)
-- 1891110180 S Dewan of Bhuj recalls meeting with Swamiji later when he was visited by a disciple (LSV 1.293)
When the Dewan of Kutch was visited by a disciple of the Swami several years later, he (the Dewan) recalled the Swami's prodigious intellect, his gracious personality, and his power of presenting abstruse thought in such a simple way that all who met him were fascinated.
-- 1891110190 S Swamiji was introduced to the Maharaja of Kutch by the Dewan. The long talks which the Swamiji had with the Maharaja made a great impression on the later. (LSV 1.293)
-- 1891110200 CE Swamiji visited the places of pilgrimage in the vicinity, mingling with the pilgrims and sannyasis, and so gaining in knowledge and experience. (LSV 1.293)
