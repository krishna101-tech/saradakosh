At Belur Math (Dec 1900)

1 6 Dec 1900 0 Swamiji arrived at Bombay. (LSV 2.558)
2 Dec 1900 0 Travelling on the Bombay Express from Bombay to Calcutta, Swamiji met his old friend Manmathanath Bhattacharya. They stared at each other for a moment in astonishment, and then entered into joyous conversation. (LSV 2.558)
3 9 Dec 1900 0 Swamiji reaches Belur Math surprisingly without prior information (LSV 2.558)
