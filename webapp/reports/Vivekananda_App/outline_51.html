56 Sep 1893 S After seventeen days of long, sometimes tiresome, sometimes stirring sessions, the Parliament of Religions came to a close. Every shade of religious thought had been expressed, from the most light filled to the most clouded, and it was not lost upon the audience that the heights of spiritual expression came from the least expected quarters. Indeed, many a Christian felt the whole affair had been disastrous. (ND 1.132)
57 27 Sep 1893 S Last and closing scene of the great Parliament of Religions. (ND 1.133)
