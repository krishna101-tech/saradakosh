Last days with Sri Ramakrishna (Aug 1886)

1 Aug 1886 S Now that the last days were approaching, Shri Ramakrishna set himself with greater enegy to mould spiritual life of boys particularly of Naren. (LSV 1.181)
2 Aug 1886 I A significant incident that took place at Cossipore during this period is known from a letter which J. J. Goodwin wrote to Mrs. Bull on May 23, 1897. (LSV 1.182)
3 Aug 1886 S Naren was initially hesitant but Master compelled him to take responsibilities to teach others. (LSV 1.182)
4 Aug 1886 I Just three or four days before the Mahasamadhi Master called Naren and transmitted all his spiritual powers through meditation to him for the good of the world. (LSV 1.182)
5 Aug 1886 I A couple of days before the Master's passing away, Master replied to the curious thought occurred in Naren's mind wether master is an incarnation of God. (LSV 1.183)
6 15 Aug 1886 I The last two days were sad ones for the disciples. Shri Ramakrishna entered into Samadhi at late night. (LSV 1.183)
7 16 Aug 1886 I The trance into which Shri Ramakrishna had entered on the previous night thus proved to have been Mahasamadhi, from which there was no return. (LSV 1.183)
8 16 Aug 1886 I Thus the curtain had fallen on a great spiritual life. (LSV 1.185)
9 16 Aug 1886 I On the same day the Holy Mother had devine vision of Shri Ramakrishna forbiding her removing her ornaments. (LSV 1.185)
10 16 Aug 1886 I A funaral procession was taken out in tha afternoon and last rights were performed at Kashipur burning ghat on the bank of Ganga. Devoteees assembled in large numbers. (LSV 1.185)
11 16 Aug 1886 I The ashes and other remains of the body were collected and put into an urn.Thereafter the Master's relics were worshipped daily by the disciples in the garden house until they were removed elsewhere. (LSV 1.186)
12 Aug 1886 S Grief stricken disciples were not in position to accept the fact that their master has left them forever.Bereavement was transformed into the ecstasy they had so often felt while the Master was alive. (LSV 1.186)
