Punjab: With the Western disciples (June 1898)

1 14 Jun 1898 S Swamiji and party reached Punjab by train. (LSV 2.356)
2 Jun 1898 S Passing through Ludhiana, Lahore, and across the Doab, they arrived at Rawalpindi. (LSV 2.356)
3 15 Jun 1898 S From Rawalpindi by tonga to Murree, reaching on June 15, where Swamiji and party stayed for three days. (LSV 2.356)
