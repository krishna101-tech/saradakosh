To Chicago (1 Jan-18 Jan 1895)

1 1 Jan 1895 CE It was in Chicago, however, that Swamiji opened the year of 1895. (ND 2.270, 3.012)
2 Jan 1895 CE Swamiji may not have stayed with the Hale family during these January weeks in Chicago. (ND 3.013)
3 Jan 1895 CE Meanwhile, plans were being made in New York for his coming classes and parlor lectures by two groups of Swamiji's friends. (ND 3. 014)
4 Jan 1895 CE About Mrs. Sara Chapman Bull. (ND 3. 014)
5 Jan 1895 CE One does not know what well-meaning plans the Vedanta Society was making for Swamiji's work. (ND 3.015)
6 Jan 1895 CE About Leon Landsberg. (ND 3.015)
7 1895 Letter Swamiji's letter to BRAHMANANDA From USA (CW SV 6.314)
