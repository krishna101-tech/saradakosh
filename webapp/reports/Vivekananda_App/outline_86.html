The Last Days (Mar-July 1902)

1 8 Mar 1902 0 Swamiji returns to Belur Math with Shivanandaji, Niranjan mj and others, to participate in Thakur tithi Puja (LSV 2.)
2 Mar 1902 Letter Swamiji's letter to BULL MRS.OLE From BELUR MATH (CW SV 9.182)
3 11 Mar 1902 0 Thakur tithi Puja. Swamiji couldn’t join the festivities due to serious relapse in health (LSV 2.)
4 11 Mar 1902 Conversations & dialogues Conversations with Sharat Chandra Chakravarti at The Belur math - 23 (CW SV 7.239)
5 1902 0 At the earnest entreaty of Niranjan mj and other guru bhais, Swamiji agreed to place himself under the treatment of an ayurvedic practitioner, Kaviraj Mahananda sen gupta of Calcutta. He was put under rigorous treatment. After more than two months' use of the Ayurvedic medicines, he felt greatly benefited (LSV 2.)
6 1902 0 Swamiji has repeatedly said, "এমন সব ভাব দিযে গেলাম, যাতে দূশো বছরের মধ্যে আর কাউকে কিছু করতে হবে না। কেবল দাগা বূলেযে গেলেই চলবে।" (SASP 001)
7 1902 0 Swamiji has repeatedly said, "এতো খেটেখূটে মন প্রস্তূত হলো, কিন্তূ মা কেবল বলছেন, 'চলে আয- চলে আয।' কাজের কাজ কিছূই করা হলো না।" (SASP 001)
8 1902 Conversations & dialogues Conversations with Sharat Chandra Chakravarti at The Belur math - 24 (CW SV 7.244)
9 1902 Conversations & dialogues Conversations with Sharat Chandra Chakravarti at The Belur math - 25 (CW SV 7.247)
10 1902 Conversations & dialogues Conversations with Sharat Chandra Chakravarti at The Belur math - 26 (CW SV 7.252)

12 1902 Conversations & dialogues Conversations with Sharat Chandra Chakravarti at The Belur math (from Calcutta to the Math) - 28 (CW SV 7.259)
13 29 Mar 1902 0 Work on the foundation of river embankment started at the instance of the Swamiji (LSV 2.)
14 30 Mar 1902 Letter Swamiji's letter to SISTER CHRISTINE From BELUR MATH (CW SV 9.181)
15 2 Apr 1902 Letter Swamiji's letter to MACLEOD JOSEFINE From BELUR MATH (CW SV 9.182)
16 2 Apr 1902 0 Rev. Oda, the abbot of a Buddhist monastery in Japan, arrived in Calcutta and stayed as the guest of Miss Macleod at the American Consulate. On the same day he went with Okakura to the Math to see Swamiji to invite him to the congress of religion (LSV 2.)
17 7 Apr 1902 0 Christine reached Calcutta. She stayed with other western disciples at the American consulate. Went to meet Swamiji the next morning with Mrs. Bull (LSV 2.)
18 1902 0 About three months before his death, Swamiji made a rule that at four o'clock in the morning a hand bell should be rung from room to room to awaken the monks, and that within half an hour all should be gathered in the chapel to meditate. Whenever his health permitted-and fortunately he was comparatively well at this time-he joined in the morning meditation in the chapel (LSV 2.)
19 21 Apr 1902 Letter Swamiji's letter to MACLEOD JOSEFINE From BELUR MATH (CW SV 5.179)
20 May 1902 0 The last two months, which Swamiji passed on earth, were full of events foreshadowing the approaching end, though at the time these events passed unsuspected by those about him. Every trifling incident had its portent and a host of associations that throbbed with a peculiarly significant meaning. (LSV 2.)
21 1902 0 Some time after he had returned from Varanasi Swamiji greatly desired to see all his sanyasi disciples, and wrote to them to visit him, if only for a short time. The call went even to those beyond the seas. (LSV 2.)
22 1902 0 Withdrawn himself from all concerns. A high spiritual mood came over him and he was making ready for the death (LSV 2.)

27 1902 0 Maharaja of Bhinga offered him money to establish monastery at Varanasi (LSV 2.)
28 Jun 1902 0 Swamiji send Shivanandaji at Varanasi to start a centre (KM)
29 6 Jun 1902 0 Went to Bara Jaguli, a village in the Nadia District of Bengal, at the earnest request of his disciple Mrinalini Bose (LSV 2.)
30 12 Jun 1902 0 Returned to the Math (LSV 2.)

35 26 Jun 1902 0 Nivedita arrived Calcutta from Mayavati and took her lodging at 17 Bose Para lane, Bagh bazar (LSV 2.)
36 28 Jun 1902 0 Swamiji came to Nivedita's residence in the morning. (*)
37 1902 Conversations & dialogues Conversations with Sharat Chandra Chakravarti at The Belur math - 29 (CW SV 7.265)
38 1902 0 A week before his passing away, Swamiji consulted an almanac to see the auspicious day (LSV 2.)
39 2 Jul 1902 0 On Ekadasi day Swamiji served morning meal to Nivedita. In the end of the meal he himself poured the water over her hands, and dried them with a towel. When Nivedita protested, he replied "Jesus washed the feet of His disciples". "But that was the last time", she thought. This was their last meeting (LSV 2.)
