Punjab: Amritsar, Rawalpindi (Aug- Sept 1897)

1 20 Aug 1897 CE Reached Amritsar along with Mr and Mrs Sevier. Received at rly station with honour. But within 4 to 5 hours retired to Dharmasala a nearby hill station due to deteriorating health. Stayed there 7-8 days and met only a few people. (LSV 2.282)
2 Aug 1897 CE Returned to Amritsar for 2 days (LSV 2.282)
3 31 Aug 1897 0 Departed for Rawalpindi (pak). Received warmly but left immediately for Murree in search of health (LSV 2. 282)
4 2 Sep 1897 CE Returned to Murree (LSV 2. 282)
5 2 Sep 1897 Letter Swamiji's letter to BRAHMANANDA From AMRITSAR (CW SV 8.418)
6 Sep 1897 CE The Swami's stay in Murree was brief. There were reasons enough why he should go on a short visit to Kashmir. (LSV 2. 282)
7 Sep 1897 CE Leaving Murree on September 6 the Swami and party reached Baramula. (LSV 2. 283)
