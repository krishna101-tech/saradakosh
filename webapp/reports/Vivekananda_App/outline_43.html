Mysore State (Oct 92)

1 Oct 1892 CE Swamiji arrived in Banglore, in Mysore State (CB SV LSV 1.321)
2 Oct 1892 S In Banglore Swamiji was a guest of Dr. Palpu, the Municipal Medical Officer. (LSV 1.321)
3 Oct 1892 I Swamiji guided Dr. Palpu in his personal matter. (LSV 1.321)
4 Oct 1892 S Swamiji became acquainted with Sir K. Seshadri Iyer, the Dewan of Mysore State. He was Dewan's guest for three to four weeks. (LSV 1.321)
5 Oct 1892 I Swamiji meets Maharaja of Mysore. ( LSV 1.322)
6 Oct 1892 S Swamiji cleared the doubts of a Muslim councillor,Abdul Rahman Saheb, concerning Koran ( LSV 1.322)
7 Oct 1892 I Swamiji's bold deliberations with Maharaja of Mysore about courtiers and Dewan ( LSV 1.322)
8 Oct 1892 S Swamiji's knowledge about western music and electricity amazed everybody ( LSV 1.323)
9 Oct 1892 I Swamiji startled the assembly of pandits with his views on Vedanta ( LSV 1.323)
10 Oct 1892 I Swamiji was asked to purchase some presents by Prime Minister. He bought a cigar ( LSV 1.323)
11 Oct 1892 I Maharaja and his Prime Minister regarded Swamiji as "the man born for regeneration of India" ( LSV 1.324)
12 Oct 1892 I Swamiji's voice was recorded by Maharaja of Mysore as a remembrance ( LSV 1.324)
13 Oct 1892 I Swamiji took leave of Maharaja. On insistance Swamiji accepted a non-metallic hookah as offfering. ( LSV 1.325)
14 Oct 1892 CE Swamiji left Mysore and went to Cochin ( LSV 1.325)
