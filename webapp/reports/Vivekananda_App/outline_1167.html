Khetri: Blessing the Prince (Apr - May 93)

1 21 Apr 1893 S Swamiji reached Khetri at about nine in the evening. Blessed Raja's son (LSV1.386)
2 Apr 1893 S This time Swamiji stayed at Khetri for almost three weeks. He spent much of his time with the Raja, talking on various matters. (LSV 1.386)
3 27 Apr 1893 Letter Swamiji's letter to RAO DR.NANJUNDA From KHETRI (CW SV 6.245)
4 28 Apr 1893 Letter Swamiji's letter to DESAI HARIDAS BIHARIDAS From KHETRI (CW SV 8.292)
5 May 1893 Letter Swamiji's letter to DESAI HARIDAS BIHARIDAS From KETRI (CW SV 8.293)
6 May 1893 I Swamiji getting ready to go to Mumbai after three week's stay at Khetri. Blessed the infant prine in women's appartments in the palace (LSV 1.386)
7 10 May 1893 S Departed from khetri for Bombay with Jagmohan munshi (LSV 1.387)
