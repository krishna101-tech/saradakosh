Trichur (Nov-Dec 92)

1 Nov 1892 CE Swamiji travelled by bullock cart from Shoranur to Trichur (LSV 1.325)
2 Nov 1892 I On way swmiji took bath and stayed in a house of an officer in Education Department (LSV 1.325)
3 Nov 1892 S Afterwards, Subramanya Iyer took him to the District Hospital run by the Government, where a Dr. De Souza treated him sore throat. (LSV 1.325)
