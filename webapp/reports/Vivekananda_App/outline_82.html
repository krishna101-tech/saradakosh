Belur Math registered (Jan-Mar 1901)

1 24 Jan 1901 0 Swamiji reached Calcutta in the morning (LSV 2.581)
2 24 Jan 1901 0 Swamiji went to Belur Math in the afternoon. His return was the cause of rejoicing among his brother-disciples and his own disciples. (LSV 2.581)
3 1901 0 Swamiji was thinking of vesting the central Math with legal authority to manage property and take care of all other matters connected with growing organization. After considering several alternative plans, he at last decided to make a trust of the Math. (LSV 2.)
4 26 Jan 1901 Letter Swamiji's letter to BULL MRS.OLE From BELUR MATH (CW SV 5.154)
5 Jan 1901 Letter Swamiji's letter to RAMAKRISHNANANDA From BELUR MATH (CW SV 5.155)
6 27 Jan 1901 0 Swamiji presided over the prize-distribution ceremony at Belur Middle English School on Sunday. (LSV 2.581)
7 Jan 1901 0 In the last week of January Miss MacLeod came to Calcutta en route for Japan, and met Swamiji. (LSV 2.581)
8 30 Jan 1901 ME A trust deed in it's final form as Debottar Trust executed. Swamiji vested all the Belur Math properties in a board of trustees consisting of Raja Mj., Baburam mj, Shivanandaji, Sarat mj, Gangadhar mj, Sarada mj, Shashi mj, Advaitanandaji, Subodh mj, Kali mj and Hari mj. Latu mj declined to be a trustee. (LSV 2.582)
9 1 Feb 1901 ME First general meeting of the Belur math trustees in presence of Swamiji. Raja mj elected president and Sarat mj secretary (BC)
10 2 Feb 1901 Letter Swamiji's letter to BULL MRS.OLE From BELUR MATH (CW SV 5.155)
11 6 Feb 1901 0 Belur math trust deed registered. Swamiji himself had to go with a pleader to the Registrar's office at Howrah to Register the deed. (LSV 2.582)
12 8 Feb 1901 0 The stamped and signed copy of the trust deed was brought from the Howrah sub Registrar's office to the Math. (LSV 2.582)
13 10 Feb 1901 ME A meeting of the trustees was held at 11a.m. on Sunday to elect the President. Raja mj was elected. Baburam mj elected as Secretary. (LSV 2.582)
14 14 Feb 1901 Letter Swamiji's letter to MACLEOD JOSEFINE From BELUR MATH (CW SV 5.156)
15 17 Feb 1901 S Shivratri (LSV 2.583)
16 17 Feb 1901 Letter Swamiji's letter to MACLEOD JOSEFINE From BELUR MATH (CW SV 5.157)
17 17 Feb 1901 S Swamiji received M Jules Bois at the Math (LSV 2.583)
18 23 Feb 1901 0 Calcutta High Court decreed in favor of the Math to cancel the tax levied on the Math by Howrah Municipality (LSV 2.583)
19 24 Feb 1901 0 Celebration of Thakur tithipuja (LSV 2.585)
