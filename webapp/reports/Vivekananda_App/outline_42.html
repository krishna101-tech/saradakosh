Goa (Oct 92)

1 18 Oct 1892 CE Swamiji at Margao in Goa. (CB SV LSV 1.318)
2 Oct 1892 Letter Swamiji writes a letter to Haripad Mitra (CW SV 8.290 CB SV LSV 1.318)
3 18 Oct 1892 S Shri V. S. Sukhthankar, writes in Hindu of Jan. 19, 1964 about Swamiji's visit to Goa (CB SV LSV 1.319)
4 Oct 1892 S Swamiji's visit to Goa was to study Christian theology. Subri Naik made arrangement with his christian friend Mr. J.P.Alvares (CB SV LSV 1.319)
5 Oct 1892 S Swamiji stayed for three days in seminary and studied theological works (LSV 1.319)
6 Oct 1892 S Padres use to visit Swamiji regularly at Margao and also attended his farewell organised by Hindus (LSV 1.320)
7 Oct 1892 I Swamiji played Tabla without making faces (LSV 1.320)
8 Oct 1892 I Swamiji posed for a photograph with Mr. Naik before leaving Goa (LSV 1.320)
9 Oct 1892 CE From Margao Swamiji left by train to Dharwar and from there direct to Mysore (LSV 1.320)
