Almora for recovery (May - July 1897)

1 6 May 1897 CE Swamiji departed Calcutta for Almora. Shivanandaji, Miss Muller, Goodwin were already there by this time. Yogen mj, Shivanandaji, Niranjan mj, Latu mj. Travelled with him during various stages (LSV 2. 256)
