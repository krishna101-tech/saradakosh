Madras (Jan 1893)

1 Jan 1893 S Swamiji at Madras with Manmath babu. Introduced to the young men of 'Triplicane literary society'. Gain their admiration and followers (LSV 1.361)
2 Jan 1893 S Right from day one of arrival at Madras Swamiji was on the high road to public recognition (LSV 1.361)
3 Jan 1893 S Mr. G. G. Narasimhachari, one of the first to meet Swamiji at Madras writes his reminiscences in Brahmavadin in 1906 (LSV 1.361)
4 Jan 1893 S Mr. G. G. Narasimhachari, also has written his reminiscences in earlier issue of Brahmavadin regarding Swamiji's first introduction to Madras Public in Literary Society at Triplicane (LSV 1.362)
5 Jan 1893 S Mr. C. Ramanujachari, who devoted his life to the work started by the Swamiji in Madras, writes on Swamiji's first visit to Madras (LSV 1.362)
6 Jan 1893 S Swamiji used to satisfy the enquirers and listeners on any subject beyond religion and philosophy (LSV 1.363)
7 Jan 1893 S Swamiji's answers of questions on 'Sandhyavandana' and 'teachings of vedas' (LSV 1.364)
8 Jan 1893 S People in Madras were amazed with Swamiji's oratory exhibiting vedas and vedanta as science (LSV 1.365)
9 Jan 1893 I Swamiji shed tears looking at half starved children of fishermen on the seashore (LSV 1.365)
10 Jan 1893 S Swamiji's views on Advaitin (LSV 1.365)
11 Jan 1893 S Swamiji's views on social reforms (LSV 1.366)
12 Jan 1893 S Press reports appreciation of Swamiji's views on various subjects. Prabuddha Bharat reports Swamiji's strong support to Age of Consent Bill to which some social reformers were hesitant to support (LSV 1.366)
13 Jan 1893 S 'It was Madras that discovered Swami Vivekananda' reports Prabuddha Bharata later in 1920 (LSV 1.367)
14 Jan 1893 I Assistant Professor of Science in the Christian College, Singaravelu Mudaliar, Came to argue, but conversation with the Swami changed him into a devoted disciple. Later known as 'kidi" (LSV 1.367)
15 Jan 1893 S Swamiji's fitting reply to fun seeking class fellows and V.Subramanya Iyer (LSV 1.368)
16 Jan 1893 I Swamiji's habit to give up his things to those who admire it. Few incedences (LSV 1.368)
17 Jan 1893 I Swamiji's strange experience at Madras about psychis disturbance by some spirits and his solution (LSV 1.369)
18 Jan 1893 S Swamiji's peronality and ideas captivated hearts and minds of people at Madras. K. Vyasa Rao gives his impression (LSV 1.369)
19 Jan 1893 S Swamiji was a great amalagamation of Hindu spiritual experience with philosophical and scientific outlook of west. His disciples writes (LSV 1.370)
20 Jan 1893 S Swamiji announces his intention to go to west at Madras (LSV 1.371)
21 Jan 1893 S People of Madras welcomed Swamiji's idea of going to west and collected good amount. G.G. Narasimhachari says about this. (LSV 1.371)
22 Jan 1893 S Swamiji refused to accept the money as he said he will go to the west at Mother's will and if he goes money will come again of itself. He asked desciples to distribute the collected amount among the poor. (LSV 1.372)
23 Jan 1893 S As per Narasimhachari and also from reminiscences of Swmi Shivananda, raja of Ramnad refused to contribute even a pie for Swamiji's proposed visit of west. Swamiji was upset (LSV 1.372)
24 Jan 1893 S Swamijee meditated intensly at madras and waiting for Mother's call (LSV 1.374)
25 Jan 1893 S People in Hyderabad wanted Swamiji to visit Hyderabad. Arrangement made for Swamiji's at house of Babu Madusudan Chatterjee's house for his stay at Hyderabad. (LSV 1.374)
26 Feb 1893 CE Swamijee left Madras for Hyderabad in early February,1893 (LSV 1.374)
