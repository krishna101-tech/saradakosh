Kodungalloor

1 1892 I Swamiji at kodungalloor. Stayed under a tree near a temple. Not permitted to enter the temple (LSV 1.326)
2 1892 S A Young man tried to have some fun with Swamiji but could not do so (LSV 1.326)
3 1892 I Swamiji's argument with two princes in Sanskrit nearby Kali temple (LSV 1.326)
4 1892 I Swamiji's discussions with two princes on third day (LSV 1.326)
5 1892 I Some of the learned women of the royal family came to meet the Swami and spoke with him in chaste Sanskrit. The Swami was surprised to find women speaking Sanskrit so fluently. In no other part of India had he come across this. No doubt he was delighted to do so now. (LSV 1.326)
6 1892 S Swamiji left temple and went towards Cochin. Princes knew his name and picture in news paper after Swamiji's success in Chicago (LSV 1.327)
