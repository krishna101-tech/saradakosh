Durga Puja at the Math (May-Dec 1901)

1 12 May 1901 0 Swamiji, with Swami Sadananda, his own mother, sister, and aunt, and Ramadada's wife, reached Calcutta from Shillong. (LSV 2.590)
2 1901 0 To please his brother disciples and his own ones, Swamiji gave up his plans and lived at the monastery for seven months in comparative retiremet. Those about him tried all to restore his health like having the best medical treatment for him and diverting his mind to lighter subjects (LSV 2.)
3 1901 0 Spend time in training Brahmacharis, singing, sometimes supervising cooking, talk about regeneration of India, renunciation etc. (LSV 2.)
4 13 May 1901 Letter Swamiji's letter to BULL MRS.OLE From BELUR MATH (CW SV 9.157)
5 13 May 1901 Letter Swamiji's letter to SISTER CHRISTINE From BELUR MATH (CW SV 9.156)
6 15 May 1901 Letter Swamiji's letter to SWARUPANANDA From BELUR MATH (CW SV 5.159)
7 1901 0 Planned to visit Japan to attend congress of religion, organised by Miss. Macleod (LSV 2.)
8 1901 0 Swamiji got a number of pets, a dog, a she goat, several cows, ducks, ships, geese, an entelope, a stork and a kid. He walked around the math barefooted, only with a kaupin on. His illness was on increase (LSV 2.)

36 Oct 1901 0 four or five days before the Durga puja, Raja mj had a vision while he was siting in the Math compound facing the Ganga. He saw Mother Durga come over the Ganga from Dakshineshwar side and stop near the bilva tree in the Math compound. Swamiji also saw the Durga Puja being celebrated at the Math, and the Mother being worshiped in a Pratima. (LSV 2.)
37 8 Oct 1901 Letter Swamiji's letter to SISTER CHRISTINE From BELUR MATH (CW SV 9.166)
38 14 Oct 1901 Letter Swamiji's letter to SISTER CHRISTINE From BELUR MATH (CW SV 9.166)
39 18 Oct 1901 0 Swamiji performs first Durga puja at Belur math [18 -21] (19th according to BC). Shivanandaji at Meerat at that time. Sad because of not been able to attend puja at the math, he saw the mother in a vision (CB SV MS)
40 Oct 1901 0 mother brought from jayrambati for worship. She blessed the puja and prohibited the sacrifice of the animal. (LSV 2.)
41 1901 0 Strict scriptural observance for the Durga Puja placated the neibouring orthodox and bigoted people. (LSV 2.)
42 1901 0 Mad mami saw a thief in early morning at Nilambar babu's house and become unconscious out of fright and consequently her insanity increased many fold. Mother decided to return to Jayrambati with her. Others suggested that mami should be sent alone with a caretaker for Radhu. During evening meditation she saw Radhu suffering in the Jayrambati from the neglect of her mother. She become very anxious for Radhu and decided to go herself ( HM)
43 Oct 1901 0 Swamiji's condition again became serious. He was told to abandon even the lightest work (LSV 2.)
44 1901 0 Swamiji also had the Laxmi Puja and Kali Puja done in the image strictly according to scriptural rites. (LSV 2.)
45 1901 0 Swamiji went to Kalighat and rolled thrice on the ground before the Kali to fulfill the vow taken by his mother to cure his serious illness during childhood (LSV 2.)
46 28 Oct 1901 0 Congress holds its 17th annual session in Calcutta. Tilak and others meet Swamiji. Gandhiji couldn't meet him. (CB SV)
47 Nov 1901 0 Rev. Oda and Kakuzo Okakura meet Swamiji (CB SV)
