At Varahnagar Math after first Pilgrimage (1888)

1 1888 I Swamiji returned to the math for a short stay (LSV 1. 215)
2 1888 S At the urge of innerself, Swamiji had dim vision of missionary life, (LSV 1. 215)
3 1888 S But the brother monks were quite averse to preaching, Swamiji still emphasised. (LSV 1. 215)
4 1888 S Swamiji was convenienced about conscious preaching and resorted to it later on. (LSV 1. 215)
