Northern California: Alameda (Apr - May 1900)

1 1 Apr 1900 S
2 11 Apr 1900 0 Swamiji reaches Alameda, stayed at the home of truth (LSV 2.)
3 12 Apr 1900 Letter Swamiji's letter to AMERICAN FRIEND From ALAMEDA (CW SV 8.517)
4 13 Apr 1900 Notes Notes of Swamiji's lecture THE SCIENCE OF YOGA at Tucker Hall, Alameda, California (CW SV 7.430)
5 14 Apr 1900 S After the lecture was over, a meeting was held in an adjoining room to inaugurate the Vedanta Society of San Francisco. (LSV 2. 515)
6 14 Apr 1900 0 Vedanta Society of San Francisco founded by Swamiji (LSV 2.)
7 16 Apr 1900 Lecture Swamiji's lecture CONCENTRATION & BREATHING at Alameda (CW SV 6.037)
8 17 Apr 1900 Letter Swamiji's letter to LEGGETT FRANCIS From (CW SV 7.524)
9 18 Apr 1900 Letter Swamiji's letter to MACLEOD JOSEFINE From ALAMEDA (CW SV 6.431)
10 18 Apr 1900 Lecture Swamiji's lecture THE PRACTICE OF RELIGION in Alameda (CW SV 4.238)
11 20 Apr 1900 Letter Swamiji's letter to MACLEOD JOSEFINE From ALAMEDA (CW SV 8.518)
12 23 Apr 1900 Letter Swamiji's letter to HALE MARY From (CW SV 8.519)
13 30 Apr 1900 Letter Swamiji's letter to HALE MARY From (CW SV 8.520)
14 May 1900 0 By May Swamiji found his California work prospering. In Vedanta Societies of LA and Pasadena, meetings were being held regularly by his students. Informal study groups were formed in Oakland and Alameda (LSV 2.)
15 Notes A GREETING : Thomas Allan's reminiscenses of Swamiji's visit to Alameda (CW SV 9.331 ND 6.136)
16 1900 0 In California Swamiji made some life long followers of his teaching and supporters of his work. Notable among them were Mrs. Mead Hansbrough, Mrs. Mead Wyckoff (of LA) Mrs. Mead Allan, Mrs. Aspinall, Dr. Logan, Mr. Peterson, Mr. Wolberg, the last three were office bearers of VS of SF (LSV 2.)
17 1900 0 Received invitation from Paris to attend the Parliament of the History of Religion, also from Mr. and Mrs. Legget to join them in Paris in July (LSV 2.)
