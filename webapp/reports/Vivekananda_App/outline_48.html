Bombay and Abu: on way to Khetri (Apr 93)

1 Apr 1893 S On his way to Khetri Swamiji stopped at Bombay. At Bombay he stayed at first with a pandit. (LSV1.385)
2 Apr 1893 S Raja mj and Hari mj fortunately met Swamiji at the home of the pandit at Bombay. (LSV1.385)
3 Apr 1893 0 Swamiji on Pratap Majumadar and others who also attended Parliament of Religion. "ও সব কিছূ না - কিছূ না। যা কিছূ ব্যাপার হবে তা কেবল (নিজের বূকে হাথ রেখে) এরই জন্য।" (SASP 001)
4 Apr 1893 S Swamiji's advise to Turiyananda on selection of subject to be selected for public speech (LSV 1.385)
5 Apr 1893 CE After staying for a few days at Bombay, the Swami and Jagmohanlal started for Khetri. Brahmananda and Turiyananda went with them as far as Abu Road, and got out there. (LSV1.386)
