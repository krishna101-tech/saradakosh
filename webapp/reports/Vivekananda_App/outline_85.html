Visit to Buddhagaya and Varanasi (Jan-Feb 1902)

1 27 Jan 1902 0 Swamiji with Miss Macleod and Okakura started for the Buddha Gaya (LSV 2.)
2 Jan 1902 0 Swamiji reached Varanasi after a week stay at Buddha Gaya with Okakura and other swamis. Received by 500 enthusiastic people at the station. Shivanandaji and Niranjan mj were there at that time. Shivanandaji return to the math (feb acc. To km) (LSV 2.)
3 1902 0 Inspired by Swamiji's teachings, several young Bengalis at Varanasi, under the leadership of Charuchandra (later Sw. shubhananda) and Jaminiranjan, had formed themselves into a band towards the middle of June 1900 to serve suffering pilgrims. After meeting with Swamiji they renamed their organization "Ramakrishna Home of Service" (LSV 2.)
4 1902 0 At Varanasi Swamiji's health did not improve rather it gradually became worse and once he become seriously ill. (LSV 2.)
5 Feb 1902 0 (First week) Mrs. Bull and Nivedita arrived in India at Calcutta via Madras. Miss Macleod, who had accompanied the Swamiji to Buddha Gaya, had left his party to receive them in Calcutta. They stayed at the American Consulate (LSV 2. NOI)
