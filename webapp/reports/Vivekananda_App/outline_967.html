France (Aug - Sept 1895)

1 17 Aug 1895 CE Swamiji sets sail for Europe with Mr. Legget. They were received at Paris by Mrs. Sturges and her sister, Miss MacLeod. (ND 3.202 LSV 2.042)
2 Aug 1895 I Swamiji's reminenances of first visit to Paris. (ND 3.202)
3 Aug 1895 CE But aside from this unpleasant state of affairs, Swamiji evidently enjoyed his stay in Paris. (ND 3.204)
4 24 Aug 1895 I Swamiji's brother Mahendra Nath Datta tells about an incident in Paris. (ND 3.205)
5 26 Aug 1895 Letter Swamiji's letter to STURDY E T From PARIS (CW SV 5.094)
6 5 Sep 1895 Letter Swamiji's letter to STURDY E T From PARIS (CW SV 8.350)
7 Sep 1895 CE But though the Swamiji had come to Paris for recreation, thoughts of his work and mission were never far from his mind. (LSV 2.042)
8 9 Sep 1895 Letter Swamiji's letter to ALASINGA PERUMAL From PARIS (CW SV 5.095 LSV 2.043)
9 Sep 1895 S This letter shows the Swami as the true sannyasi, free and fearless. (LSV 2.043)
10 9 Sep 1895 ME Swamiji witnessed the marriage of Francis Leggett and Besse (or Betty) Sturges, which took place on Monday, September 9. (ND 3.205)

12 10 Sep 1895 CE Swamiji left for England. (ND 3.206)
