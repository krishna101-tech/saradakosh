Second Pilgrimage to North India (Aug - Nov 1888)

1 1888 CE Swamiji again on pilgrimage. First to Varanasi. Met pramadadas mitra. (LSV 1. 216)
2 1888 CE Swamiji at Ayodhya. (LSV 1. 217)
3 1888 CE Swamiji at Lucknow. (LSV 1. 217)
4 1888 CE Swamiji at Agra. (LSV 1. 217)
5 Aug 1888 I Swamiji and bhangi's chillum on way to Vrindavan (LSV 1. 217)
6 Aug 1888 CE Later Swamiji answered to Girishchandra Ghose's question on incident of Chillum smoking (LSV 1. 218)
7 Aug 1888 CE Swamiji later spoke to disciple on chilum incident (LSV 1. 218)
8 Aug 1888 S (Early August) swamiji at Vrindavan. Stayed at kalababu's kunj. (LSV 1. 218)
9 Aug 1888 I Swamiji at Goverdhan Hill. Had unique experience challenging his vow of not begging his food from any one. (LSV 1. 218)
10 Aug 1888 I Swamiji at Vrindavan. Had miracle like experience while taking bath at Radhakund convenincing him that he is being taken care and protected by God. (LSV 1. 219)
11 Aug 1888 S Swamiji stayed at Vrindavan to meet brother disciple (LSV 1. 219)
12 20 Aug 1888 Letter Swamiji's letter to MITRA PRAMADADAS From VRINDAVAN (CW SV 6.201)
13 Aug 1888 I Swamiji arrived at Hathras. Met Assistant Station Master Sharatchandra Gupta at Hathras Railway Station. (LSV 1.220)
14 Aug 1888 CE Shratchandra Gupta prepared to renounce and wanted to serve Swamiji. (LSV 1.221)
15 Aug 1888 CE At Hathras, Swamiji stayed with Brajenbabu and spoke to Bengali population on religion and of their motherland. (LSV 1.221)
16 Aug 1888 CE Later sharat's friend Natakrishna recalls Swamiji's stay at Hathras (LSV 1.221)
17 Aug 1888 CE Sharat expressed willimgness to move with Swamiji with begging bowl in hand (LSV 1.221)
18 Aug 1888 I Swamiji accepted Sharat as his disciple (LSV 1.222)
19 Aug 1888 S Swamiji with Sharat Gupta at Rishikesh, Sharat inexperienced of wandering had lot of hardships. Swamiji took care of him. (LSV 1.222)
20 Aug 1888 S Sharat was not afraid of even wild animals in jungle as long as Swamiji was with him. This was the force of Swamiji's character even in those days. (LSV 1.223)
21 Aug 1888 CE Swamiji set out alone for pilgrimage leaving Sharat behind on account of his ill health, but returned after 3 to 4 hours because of his bondage with him. (LSV 1.223)
22 Aug 1888 S Swamiji had to return to Hathras on account of Sharat's ill health. Subsequently Swamiji also fell ill. (LSV 1.224)
23 Nov 1888 CE (88 end) mahapurush mj. At Hathras on way to pilgrimage at Uttarakhand. Seeing Swamiji ill, he halted his journey and brought sick swamiji to the math. Shart also follwed at later date. He was initiated to Sanyas and was given name Sadananda (LSV 1.224)
