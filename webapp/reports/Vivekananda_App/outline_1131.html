Mt. Abu

1 14 Apr 1891 CE Swamiji departed for mount Abu. Visited Dilwara Temple. (LSV 1.279)
2 Apr 1891 I At Mount Abu, Some devoted followers and some European evening walkers were charmed by Swamiji's sweet voice and ecstatic song (LSV 1.279)
3 30 Apr 1891 Letter Swamiji's letter to SAHAYA GOVINDLALA From Mount Abu (CW SV 6.244)
4 Apr 1891 Letter Swamiji's letter to SAHAYA GOVINDLALA From MOUNT ABU (CW SV 6.245)
5 1891 CE Stayed in a cave for Sadhana. (LSV 1.279)
6 1891 CE Swamiji met a Muslim Vakil while staying in a cave for Sadhana at Mt. Abu. He invited Swamiji to stay at his home. (LSV 1.279)
7 CE Through the Vakil and his brother-officers of other States, the Swami made many friends in Mount Abu, including the Vakil of the Maharao of Kotah, and Thakur Fateh Singh, the Minister of that Prince. (LSV 1.280)
8 1891 CE After a few days the Muslim Vakil invited Munshi Jagmohanlal, Private Secretary to the Raja of Khetri, to meet his guest. (LSV 1.280)
9 1891 CE Jagmohanlal told the Raja of what had happened. The Raja became so eager to meet the Swami that he said, "I will go myself to see him." When the Swami heard this he went instantly to the palace, where His Highness warmly wel-comed him. (LSV 1.280)
10 4 Jun 1891 CE Raja of Khetri was much impressed during first round of discussions with Swamiji (LSV 1.281)
11 Jun 1891 CE The State Diary of Khetri indicates that From June 4, 1891 to July 18, 1891 there were about 15 meetings between Raja Ajit singh and Swamiji.Many other digitories were also present.All the meetings were at the palace of Raja at Mount Abu. (LSV 1.281)
12 Jun 1891 CE Har Bilas Singh, a noted Historan and Arya Samajist and member of Central legistative Assembly writes about his meetings with Swamiji at Mt. Abu (LSV 1.282)
13 24 Jul 1891 CE Swamiji departed from Mt. Abu with Raja Ajit Singhji for Khetri via Jaipur. Night halt at Jaipur. (LSV 1.282)
