Cambridge (Oct 1894)

1 2 Oct 1894 CE Swamiji arrived in Cambrige - home of Mrs. Bull to sit down and write down his thoughts. (LSV 1.511 ND 2.184)
2 Oct 1894 Notes Swamiji had high regards for Mrs. Bull. (ND 2.184)
3 Oct 1894 Notes All about Mrs. Bull. (ND 2.184)
4 Oct 1894 Notes Mrs. Bull offered Swamiji a place where he could write undisturbed. (ND 2.187)
5 Oct 1894 Notes Swamiji's stay at Mrs. Bull's house was not long, though it gave him a much needed rest. (ND 2.187)
