Delhi (Jan - Feb 1891)

1 Jan 1891 S In January 1891, Swamiji left his devoted brothers and journeyed on to Delhi by himself. (LSV 1.262)
2 Feb 1891 I After about 10 days, brother disciples from Meerut came to Delhi and coincidentaly met Swamiji. He made his mind clear to them about his mission. (LSV 1.262)
3 Feb 1891 I While at Delhi, Swamiji and two brother disciples were invited by Dr. Hemchandra Sen at his house for spiritual discussions. (LSV 1.263)
4 Feb 1891 CE Shortly afterwards the brother-disciples left Delhi one after another. They all went in different directions. Swamiji alone left for Alwar, the beautiful and historic land of Rajputana. Now Rajasthan. (LSV 1.263)
