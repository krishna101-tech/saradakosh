Mahabaleshwar (Apr-June 1892)

1 May 1892 CE On hearing that the Thakore Saheb of Limbdi was at Mahabaleshwar, Swamiji went Mahabaleshwar to see the prince. (LSV 1.300)
2 May 1892 S Swamiji's presence in Mahablashwar at the end of April or beginning of May 1892 is mentioned by V.R. Joshi and from personal diary of Limbdi Thakore Saheb (LSV 1.300)
3 4 May 1892 S The entries in the personal diary of Thakore Saheb : 4 and 5 May 1892: I have deeply pondered over the discussion which took place four days ago on reincarnation, and have also referred to several books on that subject. (LSV 1.301)
4 9 May 1892 S The entries in the personal diary of Thakore Saheb : 9-11th May: I am pleasantly surprised at Swami Sri Vivekananda's deep knowledge of the Shastras. Whatever knowledge of the Shastras I had, has been much increased through discussions with him. (LSV 1.301)
5 12 May 1892 S The entries in the personal diary of Thakore Saheb : 12 May: At the end of yesterday's discussion it was proved that in ancient times the caste system was based only upon Guna and Karma. . . . (LSV 1.301)
6 18 May 1892 S The entries in the personal diary of Thakore Saheb : l8th May: There was a considerable discussion on Adharma or sin. Any action contrary to the ten charac¬teristics (Lakshanas) of Dharma is to be considered as Adharma, that is, sin. Such actions are of three kinds-Bodily, oral and mental. (LSV 1.301)
7 23 May 1892 S The entries in the personal diary of Thakore Saheb : 23 May: Different dispositions of men are caused due to the effect of Sattva, Rajas and Tamas. . . . (LSV 1.301)
8 25 May 1892 S The entries in the personal diary of Thakore Saheb : 25 May: Swami Sri Dayananda has stated that Jivas, Ishvara and Maya are beginningless (Anadi), but according to Vivekananda, Prakriti and Purusha are beginningless and the effect caused by their combination is the Jiva; I believe his opinion to be better. (LSV 1.301)
9 May 1892 S Apart from discussions with the Thakore Saheb, the Swami created a sensation at Mahabaleshwar. The editor of Maratha recalls (LSV 1.301)
10 May 1892 I Swamiji had surprise meeting with Kali Maharaj at Mahabaleshwar (LSV 1.301)
11 May 1892 I Kali Maharaj describes Swamiji at his last meeting before Swamiji's departure for America (LSV 1.301)
12 Jun 1892 CE After about two and a half months at Mahabaleshwar, the Swami left for Pune (LSV 1.301)
