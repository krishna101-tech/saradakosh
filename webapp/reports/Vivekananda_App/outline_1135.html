Kathiawar 3: Junagadh, Veraval, Somnath

1 1891 CE Swamiji returned to Junagadh, rested there for a few days. Junagadh became a Head Quarter from where Swamiji visited nearby places. (LSV 1.293)
2 1891 S Swamiji then was off to Veraval and Patan Somnath, popularly known as Prabhas. (LSV 1.293)
3 1891 CE Swamiji visited the Somnath temple, the Suraj Mandir, and the new temple of Somnath built by Rani Ahalyabai of Indore; and took bath at the confluence of the three rivers. (LSV 1.294)
4 1891 S Swamiji met Maharaja of Kutch in Prabhas and had many long conversations.The prince was astonished at Swamiji's vast knowledge. (LSV 1.294)
