Khetri

1 3 Aug 1891 CE Swamiji with Maharaja stayed in Jaipur for 10 days. Left for Khetri by train on August 3,1891 via Khairthal and Kot. Reached Khetri on August 7, 1891. (LSV 1.283)
2 7 Aug 1891 CE At Khetri Swamiji has spiritual discussions with Raja of Khetri. (LSV 1.283)
3 Aug 1891 CE Swamiji gave initiation to Raja. Raja was a wonderful disciple. Swamiji was keeping in touch with him wherever he is in the world. (LSV 1.283)
4 Aug 1891 CE Swamiji was in Khetri for more than two months. (LSV 1.283)
5 4 Oct 1891 CE Swamiji went to Sikar with Raja on eve of Navratra festival. On Desera they were back in Khetri. (LSV 1.284)
6 Oct 1891 CE Swamiji resumed study of Mahabhashya from Pandit Narayandas. Bacame the teacher of pandit in many respects. (LSV 1.284)
7 Oct 1891 CE Swamiji answers Raja's question on ' What is law?' (LSV 1.284)
8 Oct 1891 CE Raja's devotion for Swamiji was marvellous (LSV 1.285)
9 Oct 1891 CE Swamiji blessed Raja with a son and heir. (LSV 1.285)
10 Oct 1891 CE Swamiji often visited houses of poorer (LSV 1.285)
11 Oct 1891 CE Incident of a singing girl in Khetri (LSV 1.285)
