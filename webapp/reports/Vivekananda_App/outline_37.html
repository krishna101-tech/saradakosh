Ahmedabad (Nov 1891)

1 Nov 1891 S In the historic city of Ahmedabad the Swami wandered about for several days, living on alms. (LSV 1.288)
2 Nov 1891 S Swamiji was finally received as a guest by Lal Shankar Umia Shankar, a sub-judge of Ahmedabad (LSV 1.288)
3 Nov 1891 S During stay at Ahmedabad Swamiji was able to add to his knowledge of Jainism, for Jaina scholars were in plenty. (LSV 1.288)
4 Nov 1891 CE After staying for a few days in Ahmedabad, Swamiji journeyed on to Wadhwan in Kathiawar (LSV 1.288)
