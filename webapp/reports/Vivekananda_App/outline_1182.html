Swamiji at the home of Lyon

1 Oct 1893 S Swamiji stayed with Mr. and Mrs. Lyon when the Parliament was in session. (ND 1. 150 LSV 1.443)
2 Oct 1893 S Cornelia Conger was only six years old in 1893, but her memories of Swamiji are vivid, and her "Memoirs of Swami Vivekananda," first appeared in. the Prabuddha Bharata of May 1956. (ND 1. 150)
3 Oct 1893 S Miss. Conger gave an informal talk to the members of Chicago Vedanta Center at the age of eighty four. (ND 1. 150)
4 Oct 1893 S Quotation from "Memoirs" written by Miss. Conger originally written for Swami Shankarananda. (ND 1. 151 LSV 1.443)
