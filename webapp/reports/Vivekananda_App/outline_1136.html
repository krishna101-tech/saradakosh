Kathiawar 2: Bhavnagar, Sihor, Junagadh, Girnar

1 Nov 1891 CE From Limbdi Swamiji left for Junagadh (LSV 1.290)
2 Nov 1891 CE On his way to Junagadh Swamiji visited Bhavnagar and Sihor by train. (LSV 1.290)
3 Nov 1891 S Swamiji arrived at Junagadh by train from Bhavnagar. Guest of Chaganlal Pandya and Haridas Viharidas Desai, the Dewan of the Junagadh State (LSV 1.290)
4 Nov 1891 S The manager of Dewan's office, C. H. Pandya, was Swamiji's staunch admirer and he writes about Swamiji. (LSV 1.290)
5 Nov 1891 S At Junagadh Swamiji spoke much of Jesus Christ and also the excellences of the Sanatan Dharma. (LSV 1.290)
6 Nov 1891 S Swamiji also visited ancient monuments and ruins in and around Junagadh. (LSV 1.291)
7 Nov 1891 S Swamiji's pilgrimage to Girnar (LSV 1.291)
8 1891 S In Girnar Swamiji sought out a solitary cave and got absorbed in spiritual practices (Sadhana) for a few days. (LSV 1.291)
9 1891 Letter Dewan Of Junagadh state took possible care of Swamiji at Girnar. This is revealed from Swamiji's letter from Girnar. (LSV 1.291)
10 1891 I From the signature to the letter just cited, it is evident that at Junagadh he gave his name as "Vivekananda". (LSV 1.292)
11 1891 S This is confirmed by Gangadhar Maharaj who was tracking down Swamiji (LSV 1.292)
12 1891 I At junagadh Swamiji had surprise meeting with Kali Maharaj (LSV 1.292)
13 1891 S Swamiji's views on spending much of his time in the palaces of princes of india or with their Dewans (LSV 1.292)
