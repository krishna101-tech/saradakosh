Himalayas (Aug-Dec 1890)

1 Aug 1890 CE Next, one sees the Swami and his brother-disciple as guests of Babu Ramaprasanna Bhattacharya in Naini Tal, which they had reached by walking through the foothills. They were there for six days. (LSV 1.249)
2 Aug 1890 I On way to almora by foot. At kakrighat under a peepul tree swamiji had one of his greatest experience in meditation. He saw unity of microcosm with macrocosm (LSV 1.249)
3 Aug 1890 Writing THE MICROCOSM AND THE MACROCOSM : Swamiji wrote about his experience in his notebook in Bengali (CW SV 9.291 LSV 1.250)
4 Aug 1890 I On way to Almora Swamiji almost fainted with hunger. A fakir of nearby cemetery saved his life. (LSV 1.250)
5 Aug 1890 I Seven years later Swamiji back in Almora, recalls the incident (LSV 1.251)
6 Aug 1890 S By end August 1890, Swamiji and Gangadhar Mj. Arrived in Almora. Met brother-disciples. Lala Badri Sah took them to his house. (LSV 1.251)
7 Aug 1890 S in Almora Swamiji had long discussions with Shri Krishna Joshi, a Sheristadar. (LSV 1.251)
8 Aug 1890 I Swamiji performed austerities in a mountain cave above the village. He was almost at climax of his spiritual exercises but impetus to work forced him out. (LSV 1.252)
9 Aug 1890 I Gangadhar Mj. Recalls this incident in later years. (LSV 1.252)
10 Aug 1890 I On returning from cave Swamiji received sad news of his sister's suicide. (LSV 1.252)
11 5 Sep 1890 S Swamiji left for Garhwal with Gangadhar Mj, sarat Mj and kripananda. On way the party was entertained at Karna Prayag by a doctor. (LSV 1.252)
12 Sep 1890 S After leaving Karnaprayag, the Swami and Gangadhar Mj fell ill, and they were all compelled to halt in a roadside rest house for about a week. (LSV 1.253)
13 Sep 1890 CE At Rudraprayag Swamiji and Gangadhar Mj had again acute fever; recovered. Sent to Srinagar. (LSV 1.253)
14 Sep 1890 CE Road to Badarikashram closed by govt. proceed now to Srinagar (Garhwal-Uttarakhand) (LSV 1.253)
15 Sep 1890 I At Srinagar stayed on the bank of Alakananda river. Met a schoolmaster (LSV 1.253)
16 Sep 1890 I From Srinagar Swamiji and brother disciple moved to Tehri. Had to adopt a method to get food. (LSV 1.254)
17 Sep 1890 I At Tehri, Swamiji met Babu Raghunath Bhattacharya, Dewan of Tehri State. Gangadhar Mj had a severe attack of bronchitis. Advised to return to the plain. (LSV 1.254)
18 Sep 1890 I From Tehri the monks went to Rajpur via masoourie. On way down to the plains. Surprise meeting with Hari mj (LSV 1.255)
19 Sep 1890 CE Down to Dehradoon and treatment of Gangadhar Mj by the civil surgeon (LSV 1.255)
20 Sep 1890 CE Some sort of shelter had therefore to be found out for Gangadhar Mj in Dehra Dun. The Swami went from house to house to ask for shelter, but no one was prepared to take them in. Ultimately, after much effort, they found shelter in the newly-built, but incomplete, house of a businessman. They somehow accommodated themselves there, but it was not good for the patient to be in that damp house. (LSV 1.255)
21 Sep 1890 S Ultimately Swamiji Could find shelter for Gangadhar Mj at the house of pleader of the town (LSV 1.256)
22 Sep 1890 CE Swamiji Could find shelter for Gangadhar Mj but he returned to stay with brother-monks (LSV 1.255)
23 Sep 1890 S At Hridaybabu's house swamiji had discussion with Christian preachers (LSV 1.256)
24 Oct 1890 S Swamiji and his remaining brother monks went to Hrishikesh after 3 weeks stay at Dehradun. Gangadhar Mj went to Meerut. (LSV 1.256)
25 Oct 1890 S Swamiji and brother disciples stayed in a hut in Hrishikesh. (LSV 1.256)
26 Oct 1890 I Swamiji was seriously ill at Hrishikesh (LSV 1.257)
27 Oct 1890 I Dewan of Tehari State passed through Hrishikesh, Met Swamiji (LSV 1.258)
28 Nov 1890 S Swamiji to hardwar alone. Performed austerities. On hearing this raja mj came to meet him from kankhal. Other brother disciples also joined from Hrishikesh. Finaly all went to Meerut to see GangadharMj. (LSV 1.258)
29 May 1898 S That was a great hour indeed, when he spoke of Buddha (LSV 2. 340)
