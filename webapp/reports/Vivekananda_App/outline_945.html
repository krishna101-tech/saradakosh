Khandava and Indore (July 1892)

1 Jul 1892 CE Swamiji in central india. At Khandava Stayed 3 weeks at Haridas Chatterjee's house (LSV 1.303)
2 1892 S Swamiji went to Indore stayed at Indore with a friend of the Dewan of Junagadh (LSV 1.303)
3 1892 S When he returned to Khandwa, many prominent people, including members of the Bengali community, met him. All were impressed with his knowledge of the scriptures and English literature. (LSV 1.303)
4 1892 S At Khandava Swamiji met Babu Akshya Kumar Ghose who later invited him in England (LSV 1.303)
5 1892 S Haridas babu later describes Swamiji's manner (LSV 1.303)
6 1892 S Haridasbabu thought of Swamiji's public lecture in Khandava but abandoned the idea after discussing with Swamiji (LSV 1.303)
7 1892 I At Khandava Swamiji spoke on Upanishads at dinner hosted by a Civil Judge in his honour. A pleader initially planned to play a critic's role but could not do so after hearing Swamiji (LSV 1.304)
8 1892 I Swamiji gave a hint at Khandava of his intetion to go to Chicago conference next year (LSV 1.304)
9 1892 I Swamiji's determination was to leave Khandava. His host gave him a letter of introduction to his brother in Bombay (LSV 1.304)
10 1892 CE Swamiji left for Bombay in last week of July 1892 (LSV 1.305)
