Return to Madras (Feb 1893)

1 17 Feb 1893 CE Swamiji retrurns to Madras. Accoarded an ovation at station by many discipls (LSV 1.378)
2 17 Feb 1893 S Swamiji continued his religious discourses and conversations at Madras (LSV 1.378)
3 17 Feb 1893 S Desciples in Madras took definite steps to raise subsriptions for Swamiji's passage to America. This is also cleared from Swamiji's letter to Dewan of Junagadh. (LSV 1.379)
4 1893 S Inspite of Swamiji being convinced on going to America, he was still in tumult over whether to go at all. (LSV 1.380)
5 Feb 1893 S At Madras swamiji saw thakur walking on the water beconing him to follow. (LSV 1.380)
6 Feb 1893 I Met Govinda Chetty, the fortune teller, to ascertain Swamiji's mother's well being and was told of a long journey in the near future to far off lands to preach religion (LSV 1.380)
7 Mar 1893 S The news reports on Swamiji in 'The Theosophist' of March 1893. (LSV 1.381)
8 Mar 1893 I Swamiji's approach to the leader of Theosophical Society for letter of introduction was turned down. (LSV 1.382)
9 Mar 1893 I Swamiji wrote to the mother for permission of journey to the west and received positive reply (LSV 1.382)
10 1893 Notes Swamiji's lecture's NOTES TAKEN DOWN IN MADRAS (1892-93) (CW SV 6.103)
11 Apr 1893 S Touching farewell to Swamiji at Madras (LSV 1.385)
12 1893 S Munshi Jagmohan came to Madras to escort Swamiji to Khetri to bless Raja's son. (LSV 1.384)
13 1893 I Munshi Jagmohan's first meeting with Swamiji at Madras (LSV 1.384)
