Kathiawar 1: Wadhwan, Limbdi (Nov 91 - Mar 1892)

1 Nov 1891 CE Swamiji arrived at Wadhwan in Kathiawar (LSV 1.288)
2 Nov 1891 S Swamiji visited old temple of Ranik Devi in Wadhwan (LSV 1.288)
3 Nov 1891 CE From Wadhwan Swamiji went to Limbdi [in first week]. (LSV 1.288)
4 Nov 1891 I At Limbdi Swamiji was in a clutch of tantrics - Saved by Thakore saheb, the Prince of the state (LSV 1.289)
5 Nov 1891 S At Prince's invitation Swamiji stayed In palace afterwards (LSV 1.289)
6 Nov 1891 S As per 'Life of Thakore saheb', published in 1896, it was from Limbdi Thakore Saheb that the Swamiji first got the idea of going to the West in order to preach Vedanta. (LSV 1.289)
