Around Chicago

1 Oct 1893 S Swamiji accepted invitations for lectures in neighboring towns. (ND 1.165)
2 Oct 1893 S Swamiji received an invitation from the Slayton Lyceum Lecture Bureau to make a tour of America. (LSV 1.441)
3 Oct 1893 S von Bergen was one of those delegates who later developed a friendship with Swamiji. (ND 1.165)
4 Oct 1893 S Swamiji visited number of places in America. (LSV 1.442)
5 Oct 1893 S One also discovers details of his itinerary. It is now known, for instance, that through October and most of November of 1893 the Swami remained in Chicago, lecturing in and around that city. (LSV 1.442)
6 Oct 1893 S Swamiji gave various lectures during his 7 to 8 weeks stay in Chicago. (LSV 1.442)

10 Oct 1893 S We need not give here the report on the lectures that appeared in the Press of the same date, for nothing additional is to be learned from it except that "Mrs. Elizabeth Boynton Herbert entertained the lecturers at her residence Thursday evening, and here the third lectures were delivered."
