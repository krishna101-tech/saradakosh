At Varahnagar Math after third Pilgrimage (May - July 1890)

1 Apr 1890 CE Swami left Varanasi for Calcutta some time in the second week of April, 1890. Back at Math Swamiji was, as ever,the guide and leader of brother disciples. (LSV 1.237)
2 10 May 1890 Letter Swamiji's letter to MITRA PRAMADADAS From BARAHNAGAR (CW SV 6.238)
3 10 May 1890 Letter Swamiji's letter to GHOSH TULSIRAM (CW SV 9.005)
4 May 1890 CE In additon to opposition of social tradition, the brotherhood was facing financial crises also. Swamiji's presence was very uch needed in this circumstances. (LSV 1.238)
5 May 1890 CE Caught in a vortex of untoward circumstances, and in great agitation of mind, the Swami sought the advice of Pramada Babu. (LSV 1.238)
6 26 May 1890 Letter Swamiji's letter to MITRA PRAMADADAS From BAGBAZAR (CW SV 6.239)
7 May 1890 S With the passing away of the two chief financial supporters of the Math, it had become very difficult to meet the expenses. The monks decided to lead wandering life. (LSV 1.240)
8 4 Jun 1890 Letter Swamiji's letter to MITRA PRAMADADAS From BAGBAZAR (CW SV 8.285)
9 6 Jul 1890 Letter Swamiji's letter to SARADANANDA From BAGBAZAR (CW SV 6.242)
10 15 Jul 1890 Letter Swamiji's letter to SARADANANDA From CALCUTTA (CW SV 9.006)
11 Jul 1890 CE Even in the midst of training his brother disciples and planning to raise funds for maintenance of Math and memorial to the master, Swamiji was longing for a flight to the Himalayas (LSV 1.241)
12 Jul 1890 CE In third week of July 1890, ultimately swamiji made up his mind to go on pilgrimage with Akhandananda and not to return til he achieves his spritual goal. He told to his brother disciples. (LSV 1.241)
13 Jul 1890 CE (3rd week, July 1890) Swamiji took leve from mother at Ghusuri for Parivrajaka life. (LSV 1.241)
