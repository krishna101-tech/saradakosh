Kolhapur (Oct 1892)

1 Oct 1892 CE Swamiji at Kolhapur [oct-early]. (CB SV)
2 Oct 1892 S The Swamiji next visited the Maharaja of Kolhapur, to whom he had a note from the Maharaja of Bhavnagar. (LSV 1.307)
3 Oct 1892 I The Maharani of Kolhapur became greatly devoted to the Swamiji and was able to persuade him to accept a new ochre cloth. (LSV 1.307)
4 Oct 1892 I Mr. Vijapurkar, in his reminiscences, has written of the Swamiji's visit to Kolhapur (LSV 1.307)
5 Oct 1892 CE After short stay at Kolhapur, Swamiji went South, Belgaum (LSV 1.307)
