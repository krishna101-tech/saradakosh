At Varahnagar Math after second Pilgrimage

1 Nov 1888 S Swamiji stayed at Varahnagar math for a whole year, except for two short visits (LSV 1.224)
2 Nov 1888 S Swamiji's days at Varahnagar Math passed in prayer, meditation, strenuous reading and infusing in his brother monks his own fire (LSV 1.225)
3 19 Nov 1888 Letter Swamiji's letter to MITRA PRAMADADAS From VARAHNAGAR (CW SV 6.202)
4 28 Nov 1888 Letter Swamiji's letter to MITRA PRAMADADAS From BAGHBAZAR (CW SV 8.283)
5 1889 Writing A PREFACE TO THE IMITATION OF CHRIST (CW SV 8.159)
6 1889 Writing Swamiji's footnotes to A PREFACE TO THE IMITATION OF CHRIST published in Prabuddha Bharata Sept 1982 (CW SV 9.292)
7 3 Feb 1889 CE Swamiji, Raja mj etc to antpur, tarakeshwar, kamarpukur and jayarambati with mother and others (LSV 1.224)
8 4 Feb 1889 Letter Swamiji's letter to MITRA PRAMADADAS From BARAHNAGAR (CW SV 6.203)
9 7 Feb 1889 Letter Swamiji's letter to GUPTA MAHENDRANATH From ANTPUR (CW SV 6.204)
10 13 Feb 1889 CE On the way to Kamarpukur the Swami had fever and vomiting. He returned to Calcutta unwell, and had homoeopathic treatment. (According to another account he did not even reach Kamarpukur because of illness.) (LSV 1.224)
11 22 Feb 1889 Letter Swamiji's letter to MITRA PRAMADADAS From BARAHNAGAR (CW SV 8.283)
12 21 Mar 1889 Letter Swamiji's letter to MITRA PRAMADADAS From BAGBAZAR (CW SV 8.284)
13 May 1889 0 swamiji at simultala for recovery. Attack of acute diarrhoea and return to Calcutta (Summer) (LSV 1.224)
14 26 Jun 1889 Letter Swamiji's letter to MITRA PRAMADADAS From BARAHNAGAR (CW SV 6.204)
15 30 Jun 1889 S Swamiji was greatly pained on prevailing social customs and inconsistances of scriptural passages. He voiced his doubts to Pramadadasbabu in a letter. He also conveyed about litigation matter of his family in the same letter. (LSV 1.225)
16 4 Jul 1889 Letter Swamiji's letter to MITRA PRAMADADAS From BAGBAZAR (CW SV 6.205)
17 14 Jul 1889 Letter Swamiji's letter to MITRA PRAMADADAS From SIMLA, CALCUTTA (CW SV 8.284)
18 7 Aug 1889 Letter Swamiji's letter to MITRA PRAMADADAS From BARAHNAGAR (CW SV 6.208)
19 17 Aug 1889 Letter Swamiji's letter to MITRA PRAMADADAS From BARAHNAGAR (CW SV 6.209)
20 2 Sep 1889 Letter Swamiji's letter to MITRA PRAMADADAS From BAGBAZAR (CW SV 6.214)
21 3 Dec 1889 Letter Swamiji's letter to MITRA PRAMADADAS From BAGBAZAR (CW SV 6.214)
22 13 Dec 1889 Letter Swamiji's letter to MITRA PRAMADADAS From BARAHNAGAR (CW SV 6.215)
23 15 Dec 1889 S Swamiji had intense desire to go on pilgrimage (LSV 1.227)
