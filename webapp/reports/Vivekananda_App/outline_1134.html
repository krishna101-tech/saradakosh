Ajmer II

1 27 Oct 1891 CE After a little over two and a half months at Khetri, the Swami felt that he must go forth into the wide world again, unattached. He left Khetri towards the end of October, and made for Ajmer, where he stayed for about three weeks. First he stayed for three or four days with Har Bilas Sarda; and later with Sarda's friend, Shyamji Krishna Varma. (LSV 1.286)
2 Oct 1891 CE Mr. Har Bilas Sarda made a note of swamiji's visit to Ajmer in November and December 1891. (LSV 1.286)
3 Oct 1891 CE After spending about two weeks in this manner at Ajmer, the Swami seems to have proceeded towards what was then the Bombay Presidency (now Gujarat and Maharashtra). (LSV 1.287)
