Paris (July-Oct 1900)

17 3 Sep 1900 Letter Swamiji's letter to LEGGETT MRS. From PARIS (CW SV 6.436)
18 3 Sep 1900 0 Congress of the History of religions held during september 3 to 8, at the Sorbonne in connection with the Paris exposition. It was concerned exculusively with scholarly matters of different doctrines or beliefs, such as had taken place so fervently at Chicago was not allowed (*)
19 1900 0 Pere Hyacinthe (Loyson) , a former carmelite monk and catholic priest, was one of Swamiji's greatest intimates during his stay in France. Duke of Richeliu was another one who become greatly attached to him. Others who were attracted by him were Hiram Maxim, of gun fame, Sarah Bernhardt, a celebrated actress, Madame Calve etc (LSV 2.)
20 Sep 1900 0 (First week) moved with M. Jules Bois, a well known writer and a student of comparative religion. Swamiji hoped that by living alone with Bois, who spoke nothing but french, he might thereby more proficient in french. Bois' apartment remained his head quarters in Paris, until he left for his European tour (LSV 2.)
21 7 Sep 1900 0 Swamiji attends a sectional meeting of the paris congress. He was present at several sittings of the Congress but his ill health prevented him from lecturing more than twice (LSV 2.)
22 7 Sep 1900 Writing THE PARIS CONGRESS OF THE HISTORY OF RELIGIONS (CW SV 4.422)
23 10 Sep 1900 Letter Swamiji's letter to STURGES ALBERTA From PARIS (CW SV 6.439)
24 15 Sep 1900 Letter Swamiji's letter to SISTER CHRISTINE From PARIS (CW SV 9.148)
25 17 Sep 1900 0 Swamiji together with Jules bois, accepted an invitation of Mrs. Bull to become her guests in a house she held taken at Perros-Guirec, a small village on the English channel. Here he gave himself up to leisure and rest. Nivedita was also there. (LSV 2.)
26 22 Sep 1900 Poem A BENEDICTION (Composed for Sister Nivedita at peroz guirec, Brittanee, France) (CW SV 6.178)
27 22 Sep 1900 Letter Swamiji's letter to STURGES ALBERTA From BRETAGNE (CW SV 7.526)
28 28 Sep 1900 0 On way to Paris, Swamiji visits Mt. St. Michael (CB SV)
29 Sep 1900 0 (last week) Returned to Paris. Here Swamiji and moved in the most distinguished circles. (LSV 2.)
30 Oct 1900 Letter Swamiji's letter to CALVE EMMA From PARIS (CW SV 8.536)
31 14 Oct 1900 Letter Swamiji's letter to SISTER CHRISTINE From PARIS (CW SV 8.537)
32 15 Oct 1900 0 Second visit of Swamiji to Perros Guirec with Jules Bois, before leaving France (LSV 2.)
33 1900 0 While he was guest of Leggetts Swamiji met many distinguished person, men and women of all branches of knowledge and culture, poets, philosophers, writers, actors, singers, painters etc. (LSV 2.)
34 22 Oct 1900 Letter Swamiji's letter to BULL MRS.OLE From RUE AMPERE (CW SV 9.149)
35 24 Oct 1900 0 Swamiji boarded famous "Oriental Express" leaving Paris for a journey across southwest Europe to Constantinopal accompanied by madame Calve, M and Mdm Loyson, Jules Bois and Miss MacLeod (LSV 2.)
