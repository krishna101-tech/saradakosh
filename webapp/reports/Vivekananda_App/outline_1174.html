Massachusets: Salem (Aug - Sept 1893)

1 28 Aug 1893 S Swamiji arrived in Salem. He had been invited by Mrs. Kate Tannatt Woods to be her house guest. (ND 1.041 LSV 1.410)
2 Aug 1893 S More information about Mrs. Kate Woods is availble from letters of her daughter in law. (ND 1.042 ND 1.064 ND1.065)
3 Aug 1893 S Mary Louis Burke visits Salem to gather further information about Swamiji's stay. (ND 1.044)
4 Aug 1893 S While Swamiji was at Breezy Meadows, His first lecture at 'Thought and Work Club' in Salem was organised by Mrs. Woods. (ND 1.049)
5 Aug 1893 S The news about Swamiji's proposed visit to Salem on 28th August, 1893 and lecture at Wesley Chapel appeared in 'Salem Evening News ' of August 24, 1893. (ND 1.046)
6 Aug 1893 S Swamiji gave two lectures and a talk in Salem. (ND 1.041 LSV 1.410)
7 28 Aug 1893 Lecture In the afternoon Swamiji spoke to members of 'Thought and Work Club' in Wesley chapel on 'India - Her Religion and Custom'. The reports came in Salem News papers on subsequent days. (ND 1.041 LSV 1.410)
8 Aug 1893 S Mrs. Prince Woods made a note on Swamiji's first encounter with hostile Christian ministers. (ND 1.050)
9 29 Aug 1893 News reports News report in Cape Ann Breeze, on Swamiji's first lecture in American Church at Annisquam (ND 1.040)
10 29 Aug 1893 News Reports News report in Salem Evening News - Swamiji's lecture on 'INDIA : HER RELIGION AND CUSTOMS at Wesley Chapel, Salem (CW SV 3.465 ND 1.046)
11 29 Aug 1893 News Reports News report on Swamiji INDIA : HER RELIGION AND CUSTOMS in Daily Gazette, dtd. 29.08.1893 (CW SV 3.467)
12 29 Aug 1893 News Reports News report in Daily Gazette - Swamiji's lecture on 'INDIA : HER RELIGION AND CUSTOMS at Wesley Chapel. (CW SV 3.467 ND 1.048)
13 29 Aug 1893 S On the breezy, prestorm afternoon, Swamiji spoke in the garden to the children, Mrs. Prince wood made note of this. The Salem Evening News of September 1 reported on this event. (ND 1.050)
14 29 Aug 1893 Letter A letter written by Mrs. Wright to her mother giving detailed account of Swamiji's stay in Annisquam during last week end of August, 1893. (ND 1.027)
15 Aug 1893 S The following letters were found among Dr. Wright's papers and, together with others written by Swamiji to the professor, have been made available to Marie Louise Burke by his son, Mr. John K. Wright: (ND 1.052)
16 30 Aug 1893 Letter Swamiji's letter to WRIGHT PROF.JOHN H From SALEM (CW SV 7.449 ND 1.052)
17 Aug 1893 S Swamiji evidently did not yet know the decision of the officials of the Parliament of Religions to whom Professor Wright had written. But by September 2, the arrangements were well under way: (ND 1.053)
18 1 Sep 1893 News Report News Report on Swamiji's talk with children on Tuesday (29th, August) in Salem Evening News (CW SV 3.468 ND 1.051)
19 2 Sep 1893 Letter Swamiji's letter to WRIGHT PROF.JOHN H From SALEM (CW SV 7.449 ND 1.053)
20 Sep 1893 S Swamiji's likely first poem in English written to Prof. Wright. (ND 1.054)
21 3 Sep 1893 S Swamiji lectured at the East Church in Salem. The Daily Gazette of September 5 reported on this event. (ND 1.050)
22 Sep 1893 S In Swamiji's letters and lectures one can trace his change of attitude through the months in his approach to the American public. (ND 1.052)
23 Sep 1893 S Certain reactions to the Swami's first lecture in Salem surely forewarned him of the powerful opposition he would have to face in months to come. (LSV 1.410)
24 Sep 1893 S Two years later Ella Wheeler Wilcox writes to Mrs. Woods of Swamiji. (ND 1.043)
25 Sep 1893 S Mrs. Prince woods writes about Swamiji's gracious gesture in giving his precious possessions of staff, trunk and blanket to Mrs. Wood's family. (ND 1.041)
26 4 Sep 1893 S Swamiji left Salem at night for Saratoga Springs, NewYork (ND 1.055)
