Northern California: Camp Tylor and San Fransisco (May 1900)

1 2 May 1900 0 Swamiji reaches camp Taylor, Marine county to rest after the exhaustion of the work and a severe set back in health at Alameda (LSV 2.)
2 2 May 1900 Letter Swamiji's letter to SISTER NIVEDITA From (CW SV 8.521)
3 2 May 1900 Letter Swamiji's letter to BLODGETT MRS. From (CW SV 7.525)
4 Notes THIS WORLD IS A CIRCUS RING : Mrs. Alice Hansbrough's reminiscense of Swamiji in Camp Tylor (CW SV 9.331 ND 6.136)
5 16 May 1900 0 (16 or 17) Returned to San Francisco. He was not yet restored to health thus it was decided that he stay at the residence of his disciple Dr. Logan under constant medical care and observation (LSV 2.)
6 17 May 1900 Letter Swamiji's letter to SISTER NIVEDITA From SAN FRANCISCO (CW SV 9.136)
7 18 May 1900 Letter Swamiji's letter to BULL MRS.OLE From SAN FRANCISCO (CW SV 9.137)
8 18 May 1900 Letter Swamiji's letter to SISTER NIVEDITA From SAN FRANCISCO (CW SV 9.137)
9 19 May 1900 Letter Swamiji's letter to ABHEDANANDA From SAN FRANCISCO (CW SV 9.138)
10 19 May 1900 Letter Swamiji's letter to SISTER CHRISTINE From SAN FRANCISCO (CW SV 9.138)
11 24 May 1900 Notes Frank Rhodehamel's notes of Swamiji's Bhagavad GITA class delivered on Thursday in San Francisco (CW SV 9.274 ND 6.175-76)
12 24 May 1900 Questions & Answers San Franscisco - IN ANSWER TO NIVEDITA (CW SV 5.320)
13 26 May 1900 Lecture Swamiji's lecture GITA 1 26.05.1900 at San Franscisco (CW SV 1.446)
14 26 May 1900 Notes Frank Rhodehamel's notes of Swamiji's Bhagavad GITA 1 class delivered on Saturday in San Francisco (CW SV 9.276 ND 6.205)
15 26 May 1900 Letter Swamiji's letter to SISTER NIVEDITA From SAN FRANCISCO (CW SV 8.522)
16 28 May 1900 Lecture Swamiji's lecture GITA 2 28.05.1900 at San Franscisco (CW SV 1.459)
17 29 May 1900 Lecture Swamiji's lecture GITA 3 29.05.1900 at San Franscisco (CW SV 1.467)
18 29 May 1900 Notes Frank Rhodehamel's notes of Swamiji's lecture GITA 3 delivered on Tuesday in San Franscisco (CW SV 9.278, 1.467 ND 6.213)
19 1900 Notes REMARKS FORM VARIOUS LECTURES : Frank Rhodehamel's random lecture notes of Swamiji (CW SV 9.282 ND 6.209)
20 30 May 1900 0 Swamiji leaves San Francisco for Chicago en route to New York (LSV 2.)
