Alwar (Feb 1891- Oct 1891)

1 Feb 1891 I (Early February,1891) Swamiji proceeding to Rajputana, Land of heroes (LSV 1.264)
2 Feb 1891 I (Early February,1891) Swamiji at Alwar. First day at Alwar (LSV 1.264)
3 Feb 1891 CE In a few days Number of devotees increased so much that Swamiji had to shift to the house of Pandit Shambhunathji. (LSV 1.265)
4 Feb 1891 CE Swamiji was getting much involved when the talks were centerd on Mother-Worship (LSV 1.266)
5 Feb 1891 CE Some days Swamiji would sing of Shri krishna's life in Vrindavan and was getting much involved. (LSV 1.266)
6 Feb 1891 CE This was the routine. Some times meetings would continue till mid night. (LSV 1.267)
7 Feb 1891 CE One of the devotee Moulvi Saheb entertained Swamiji at his house for food. Other Muslims followed Moulavi's example. (LSV 1.267)
8 Feb 1891 I Dewan of Alwar invited Swamiji to his house with an intesion to have good infuence on much anglicized Maharaja. (LSV 1.268)
9 Feb 1891 CE Maharaja of Alwar opened the conversation with Swamiji (LSV 1.268)
10 Feb 1891 I Incident of immage worship - Swamiji asked Dewan and other people to spit on picture of Maharaja of Alwar. (LSV 1.268)
11 Feb 1891 CE Maharaja of Alwar was very much impressed and asked Dewan to keep Swamiji with him. Swamiji accepted the invitation conditionaly (LSV 1.270)
12 Feb 1891 I In one incedent Swamiji exhibited his firmness (LSV 1.270)
13 Feb 1891 I Swamiji's advise to house holders to help a Brahmin boy to have his thread ceremony (LSV 1.272)
14 Feb 1891 CE Swamiji explains to young men of Alwar why Indian should write Indian history ? (LSV 1.271)
15 Mar 1891 I In Alwar swamiji met an old Brahmachari of typical nature and grumpy. (LSV 1.273)
16 Mar 1891 I Swamiji's visit to the home of a disciple (LSV 1.273)
17 28 Mar 1891 CE Swamiji left for Pandupol (LSV 1.275)
18 At Jaipur the disciple insisted on the Swami's posing for a photograph. The Swami, much against his wishes, finally consented. This was the first time that a photo of him as a wandering monk was taken.
