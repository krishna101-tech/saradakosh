RKM plague relief (May 1898)

1 3 May 1898 S On receiving the news of plague outbreak in Calcutta Swamiji returned to Culcutta. (LSV 2. 304 LSV 2.328)
2 3 May 1898 Writing PLAGUE MENIFESTO : On the day of his return to Culcutta Swamiji drafted plague menifesto in Hindi and Bengali.He wanted to start relief operations immediately to help the afflicted. (CW SV 9.300 LSV 2. 328)
3 May 1898 S Swamiji ready to sale the Math land to raise money for plague relief. Fortunately the fund soon begin to arrive. (LSV 2. 328)
4 May 1898 S It was settled that an extensive plot of ground should be rented at once, on which segregation camps would be set up in compliance with the Government’s plague regulations. (LSV 2. 328)
5 7 May 1898 0 Dharmapala visits Swamiji at Belur Math (CB SV)
6 1898 S The Swami remained in Calcutta until the possibility of an epidemic had passed and the stringent plague regulations had been withdrawn. (LSV 2.330)
