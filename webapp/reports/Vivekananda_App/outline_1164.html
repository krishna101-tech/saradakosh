Abu: on way to Bombay (May 93)

1 16 May 1893 I Swamiji stopped at Abu road. Raja mj and Hari mj came to meet him there (LSV 1.387)
2 May 1893 I Hari Mj. Relates how Swamiji's heart has expanded very much. (LSV 1.387)
3 May 1893 I Hari Mj. Relates an incident indicative of the profound love for all men that there was in Swamiji's heart. (LSV 1.387)
4 May 1893 S On the eve of leaving them, Swamiji said to Turiyananda, "You leave Brahmananda alone and go to the Math. Do the Master's work there and try for the improvement of the Math." (LSV 1.387)
5 May 1893 I Swamiji had an unpleasant experince with European Ticket Collector at Abu Road station. (LSV 1.389)
