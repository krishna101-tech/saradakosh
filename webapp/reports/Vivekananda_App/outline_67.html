Darjeeling (Mar-Apr 1898)

1 30 Mar 1898 S Left for Darjeeling with Nirbhayananda and Nityagopal ghosh, guest of M N Banerjee. Tried to follow the instruction of physicians not to think on any serious subject. (LSV 2.326)
2 3 Apr 1898 Lecture Swamiji lectured on Hinduism in Darjeeling Hindu hall with shri A.C. Bose in chair. (LSV 2. 327)
3 4 Apr 1898 Letter Swamiji's letter to BULL MRS.OLE From DARJEELING (CW SV 9.102 LSV 2.327 ND 5.059)
4 7 Apr 1898 S Miss Müller, with Swami Akhandananda, reached Darjeeling to have the company of Swamiji. They stayed separately in a bungalow called "Rose Bank", where Mr. and Mrs. Sevier had stayed the preceding year. It was about a mile and a half from where the Swami was staying. (LSV 2.327)
5 Apr 1898 S Daily, in the morning, Swamij would to go to "Rose Bank" for coffee. After spending two or three hours there, he would return to his place with Swami Akhandananda. After lunch and the noon rest, Akhandananda would return to "Rose Bank". (LSV 2.327)
6 Apr 1898 S On Easter Sunday, 1898, Sister Nivedita wrote in reply to Swami Akhandananda, at Darjeeling: (LSV 2.327)
7 1898 S While at Darjeeling the Swami went to Sandukphu [3634 m] and other places, to see the snow. He was then in good health. (LSV 2.327)
8 1898 S Swamiji got fever and then cough and cold after returning to Darjeeling (LSV 2.327)
9 1898 S When the Swami was only partially restored to health, news reached him of the outbreak of plague in Calcutta. (LSV 2.328)
