Kanya Kumari (Dec 1892)

1 23 Dec 1892 S Swamiji at Kanya kumari (LSV 1.340)
2 Dec 1892 I Swamiji Worshipped Manmath babu's little daughter as virgin goddess (kanya kumari). Parted company from him (LSV 1.340)
3 Dec 1892 I Swamiji swam across the sea for about two furlong and reached the holy rock for meditation (LSV 1.341)
4 24 Dec 1892 I Swamiji passed into a deep meditation sitting on last stone of India at Kanya kumari (LSV 1.341)
5 Dec 1892 S After meditation on last stone of India, Swamiji got tranformed in to a reformer, a nation builder and a world architect (LSV 1.341)
6 Dec 1892 S Swamiji meditated on rock for three days (LSV 1.344)
7 Dec 1892 S Swamiji's letter of March 19,1894 to Swami Ramkrishnananda from Chicago reveals the ardour of his meditation on rock (LSV 1.342)
8 Dec 1892 S At Kanya kumari Swamiji became the patriot and prophet in one! (LSV 1.343)
9 Dec 1892 S Meditation confirmed Swamiji's intention of going to the west (LSV 1.343)
10 Dec 1892 S Swamiji left for Rameshwaram on foot (LSV 1.345)
