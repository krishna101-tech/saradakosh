Third Pilgrimage to North India (Dec 1889 - May 1890)

39 Mar 1890 CE Swamiji's resolution of pilgrimage to North India was delyed because of his as well as brother disciples's ill health. He wanted to learn Hath yoga from Pavhari baba to over come this problem. But the Babaji was proving difficult and show no inclination to pass on his knowledge to Swamiji. (LSV 1.233)
40 Mar 1890 CE Swamiji, whenever decided to take initition from Pavhari Baba, he was getting visons from Shri Ramkrishna and ultimately swamiji gave up the idea of initiation altogether. (LSV 1.233)
41 Mar 1890 CE Ultimately Shri Ramkrishna triumphed. Swamiji later composed a song in Bengali entitled "A song I sang to thee". (LSV 1.234)
42 Mar 1890 CE Swamiji had great impact of the vision of Shri Ramkrishna in his mind (LSV 1.234)
