Kutch, Bhuj

1 1891 S Swamiji departed for Bhuj. He Stayed at Dewan's house. (LSV 1.293)
2 1891 S Swamiji had long discussions with Deawn, on the industrial, agricultural and other economic problems of the country, and on the need for the spread of education among the masses. (LSV 1.293)
3 1891 S Dewan of Bhuj recalls meeting with Swamiji later when he was visited by a disciple (LSV 1.293)
4 1891 S Swamiji was introduced to the Maharaja of Kutch by the Dewan. The long talks which the Swamiji had with the Maharaja made a great impression on the later. (LSV 1.293)
5 1891 CE Swamiji visited the places of pilgrimage in the vicinity, mingling with the pilgrims and sannyasis, and so gaining in knowledge and experience. (LSV 1.293)
