Meetings with Mme Calve & Rockefeller

1 Mar 1894 S Meeting with Mme calve. (ND 1.483)
2 Mar 1894 S There is yet another story which Mme Calve told regarding this period of Swamiji's life. Unfortunately we are not in a position to authenticate it, but it is not in essence an unlikely story, and at the risk of providing material for the start of a legend I think it should be made available to Swamiji's followers. It is related in Mme Verdier's journal from notes taken during conversations with Mme Calve. (ND 1.486)
