Trivendrum

1 13 Dec 1892 CE Swamiji arrived at Thiruvanthpuram (Trivandrum). (LSV 1.330)
2 13 Dec 1892 CE In Trivandrum, the capital of Travancore, Swamiji stayed with Prof. Sundararama Iyer, who was tutor to the First Prince, the nephew of the Maharaja of Travancore, for nine days. (LSV 1.330)
3 13 Dec 1892 I Prof. Sunderrama Iyer recalls his first meeting with Swamiji (LSV 1.330)
4 13 Dec 1892 I Prof. writes about Swamiji's meeting in the evening at Trivendrum Club (LSV 1.331)
5 13 Dec 1892 I Swamiji teaches manners to Dewan Peshkar at Trivendrum Club. (LSV 1.331)
6 14 Dec 1892 I Next day Swamiji paid a visit to Prince of Travancore and mentioned to him about his visits to various native prince and courts during his travels. (LSV 1.332)
7 14 Dec 1892 I At the insistance of Prince Swamiji met Maharaja of Travancore but little disappointed (LSV 1.332)
8 14 Dec 1892 I The prince, being an amature photographer, took picture of Swamiji and sent an exhibit to the next Fine Arts Exhebition held in Madras Museum (LSV 1.332)
9 Dec 1892 S Swamiji sometimes used to criticize prof.Iyer's orthodox way of life (LSV 1.333)
10 Dec 1892 I Swamiji's views on Science and Religion (LSV 1.333)
11 Dec 1892 I Swamiji's views on Laukika and alaukika (LSV 1.333)
12 17 Dec 1892 I Swamiji's views on Indriya-Nigraha (LSV 1.334)
13 17 Dec 1892 S Swamiji met Manmath Bhattacharya Dy. Accountant General of Madras (LSV 1.334)
14 Dec 1892 I Swamiji's discource on Advaita Vedanta (LSV 1.335)
15 Dec 1892 I Swamiji on public lecture (LSV 1.336)
16 Dec 1892 I Swamiji gave his views on Indian Ethnology (LSV 1.336)
17 Dec 1892 S Swamiji's stay with prof. Sundarama Iyer left impressions in his mind (LSV 1.337)
18 Dec 1892 S Shri S. K. Nair of Travancore writes of association of Prof. Rangacharya and Prof. Sundarama Iyer with Swamiji (LSV 1.337)
19 Dec 1892 S Shri K. S. Ramaswami Shastri, the son of Prof. Sundararama Iyer gives his reminiscences regarding Swamiji's views on serving fellow countrymen (LSV 1.338)
20 Dec 1892 S Swamiji left everlasting impression in the heart of all members of host's house (LSV 1.336)
21 22 Dec 1892 I Swamiji's last discussion on Sanskrit grammer just at the time of leaving (LSV 1.337)
22 22 Dec 1892 S Swamiji's departure from Trivendrum to Kanyakumari. (LSV 1.339)
