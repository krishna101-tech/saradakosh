Kathiawar 4: Junagadh, Porbandar

1 1891 CE Swami returned to Junagadh. This place seems to have been a centre from which he made a number of side trips through Kathiawar and to Kutch. Leaving Junagadh a third time, he now went to Porbandar with a letter of introduction to the Dewan. (LSV 1.294)
2 1891 CE Porbandar is held to be the site of the ancient city of Sudamapuri, known to readers of the Bhagavta. Here the Swami visited the ancient temple of Sudama. (LSV 1.294)
3 1891 S At Porbandar Swamiji stayed with Dewan. Helped him in his translation of Vedas. (LSV 1.294)
4 1891 S At Porbandar, Swamiji finished his reading of Panini’s Mahabhashya, and took up the study of French at the instance of the Dewan, who said, "It will be of use to you, Swamiji." (LSV 1.295)
5 1891 S The Dewan of Porbandar advised Swamiji to go to western countries and preach Sanatan Dharma there. This advise coincides with his views as well as of C. H. Pandya, the manager of Dewan's office at Junagadh. (LSV 1.295)
6 1891 S Swamiji was exceedingly restless. He was beginning to feel the truth of the Master's words, that he had power enough in him to revolutionize the world. He must first preach the glories of the Sanatana Dharma to the West. (LSV 1.295)
7 1891 I At Porbandar Swamiji met Sharada Maharaj in curious circumsatances (LSV 1.296)
8 1891 S A centenarian named Acharya Revashankar Anupam Dave reminisces about Swamiji (LSV 1.296)
