Nadiad, Baroda (Mar - Apr 1892)

1 Mar 1892 CE Swamiji came to Nadiad. Met Haridas Desai's relatives (LSV 1.300)
2 Mar 1892 CE The Swami next went to Baroda, the capital of the Gaekwad of Baroda. He stayed for a short time with Bahadur Manibhai J., one of the ministers of the State and a man of piety and noble character. (LSV 1.300)
3 26 Apr 1892 Letter Swamiji's letter to DESAI HARIDAS BIHARIDAS From BARODA (CW SV 8.286 LSV 1.300)
4 26 Apr 1892 CE Swamiji at Baroda leaves for Bombay (LSV 1.300)
