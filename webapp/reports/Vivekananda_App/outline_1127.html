Baidyanath, Gazipur, Varanasi, Ayodhya (July-Aug 1890)

1 Aug 1890 S Next halt of Swamiji and Gangadhar Mj. Was at Vaidyanath. Met Rajnarayan Bose - the Brahmo preacher, Veteran social reformer and national leader. (LSV 1.247)
2 Aug 1890 CE From Vaidyanath they went to Ghazipur and from there to Varanasi and stayed with Pramadadasbabu for few days. (LSV 1.248)
3 Aug 1890 S Gangadhar Mj. Brought the ticket for Ayodhya against Swamiji's wish to meet Janakibal Sharma - Mahant of a temple. Swamiji was impressed after meeting Mahant. (LSV 1.249)
