With Thakur at Shyampukur (Sept 1885 - Dec 1885)

1 26 Sep 1885 S Accordingly a small house was rented at Durgacharan Mukherjee Street in Baghbazar, but the Master did not like it and straightaway left for Balaram Bose's house and stayed there for a week.. ((*)LSV 1.151)
2 Sep 1885 S Within a week Shri Ramakrishna was moved to a better house at 55 Shyampukur Street in Shyampukur. (LSV 1.151)
3 Sep 1885 S Naren made all arrangements.He influenced the other disciples who forgot studies and home and resolved to be in service of their Master. (LSV 1.151)
4 1885 S Disciples went on overcoming all related difficulties and took the opportunity to serve their master. (LSV 1.151)
5 1885 S Though there were much speculation among the devotees as to the reason for the Master's illness, all were convinced beyond doubt that the goal of life would be realized if they could follow his example and render him service. (LSV 1.151)
6 1885 S Naren saw In Shri Ramakrishna the human and the divine were inseparable. (LSV 1.152)
7 18 Oct 1885 0 With Suren, Navagopal, Naren, Girish etc at Shyampukur (KTM 2.848)
8 23 Oct 1885 0 With dr. Sarkar, Naren, Girish etc at Shyampukur (KTM 2.868)
9 24 Oct 1885 0 With Naren, Dr. Sarkar, Mahima charan etc at Shyampukur (KTM 2.876)
10 25 Oct 1885 0 With Vijay, Naren, Dr. Sarkar at Shyampukur (KTM 2.879)
11 27 Oct 1885 S About Shri Ramakrishna, Naren said,"I do not say that he is God. What I am saying is that he is a Godlike man. Hence we offer him worship." (LSV 1.152)
12 27 Oct 1885 0 With Naren, Dr. Sarkar, Girish etc at Shyampukur (KTM 2.895)
13 1885 S Shri Ramakrishna's teachings at Shyampukur were replete with the spirit of renunciation. (LSV 1.153)
14 1885 S Doctor attending Shri Ramakrishna was very much impressed with Naren's conversation and singing. (LSV 1.153)
15 1885 S Naren had many leadership qualities including power of discrimination and comprehensive outlook on life. (LSV 1.153)
16 1885 S Shri Ramakrishna's teachings were different to different disciplesdepending on their caliber. This entailed individual training. (LSV 1.154)
17 1885 S Girish Chandra Ghose openly proclaimed Shri Ramakrishna as incarnation of God. (LSV 1.154)
18 Oct 1885 I A Brahmo preacher Vijay Krishna Goswami had a vision of Master as he sat in meditation in his room at Dacca. He came all the way to Kolkatta. (LSV 1.155)
19 Oct 1885 I With Vijay Krishna's presence in Shyampukur, belief in the Shri Ramakrishna as a divine Incarnation spread among the devotees like wild fire. (LSV 1.155)
20 1885 S Narendranath realzing the dangersof the path the devotees were taking, tried to warn them (LSV 1.156)
21 1885 S Naren was busy inpersuading the brother disciples in his own spiritual persuit and in shaping them. (LSV 1.157)
22 29 Oct 1885 0 With Naren, Dr. Sarkar, etc at Shyampukur (KTM 2.907)
23 30 Oct 1885 0 With Naren, Dr. Sarkar, Pratap etc at Shyampukur (KTM 2.912)
24 31 Oct 1885 0 With Naren, Mishra, Harivallabh, etc at Shyampukur (KTM 2.920)
25 11 Dec 1885 S Shri Ramakrishna was moved to Kashipur Garden House (LSV 1.157)
