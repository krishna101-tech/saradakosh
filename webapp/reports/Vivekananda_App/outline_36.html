Belgaum (Oct 92)

1 8 Oct 1892 CE Swamiji at Belgaum at the house of Haripada Mitra [8-17]. (arrived on Oct.15 according to LSV) (CB SV)
2 15 Oct 1892 CE The Swamiji came to Belgaum from Kolhapur with a note from Mr. Golvalkar, the Khajgi Karbhari [Private Secretary] of the Maharaja. (LSV 1.308)
3 15 Oct 1892 S Swamiji was a guest of a Mahrashtrian gentleman, whose son Prof. G.S Bhate recalls later (LSV 1.308)
4 Oct 1892 S Prof. G.S. Bhate records about Swamiji's daily life (LSV 1.309)
5 Oct 1892 I Swamiji asked for pan and supari at the home of Prof. G.S. Bhate (LSV 1.309)
6 Oct 1892 I Prof.G.S.Bhate writes on Swamiji's knowledge of Sanskrit (LSV 1.310)
7 Oct 1892 S As per records of Prof. G. S. Bhate, initial small get to gether of local leaders and lerned men turned out in to a crowded gatherings every day and Swamiji became very popular in Belgaum (LSV 1.310)
8 Oct 1892 I An executive engineer argues with Swamiji concerning belief in religion at the home of Prof. G. S. Bhate (LSV 1.310)
9 18 Oct 1892 I Haripada babu met Swamiji for the first time. (LSV 1.310)
10 19 Oct 1892 I Haripada babu met Mr. Bhate and took Swamiji to his house (LSV 1.311)
11 Oct 1892 I After 3 days Swamiji wanted to leave but at the request of Haripada babu consented to stay for few days more (LSV 1.311)
12 Oct 1892 I Swamiji told many incidents of his wandering life to Haripadababu (LSV 1.312)
13 Oct 1892 I Swamiji tells to Haripada babu about his ability to memorise in two readings only (LSV 1.312)
14 Oct 1892 I Haripada Babu recalls Swamiji as a real teacher (LSV 1.313)
15 Oct 1892 I Swamiji's views on medicines helps Haripada babu to give up habit (LSV 1.313)
16 Oct 1892 I Swamiji's advise made an indelible impression on Haripadababu and he turned over a new leaf (LSV 1.314)
17 Oct 1892 S Swamiji explained Bhagavad Gita to Haripadbabu (LSV 1.314)
18 Oct 1892 I Swamiji was upset on hearing the news about a death of a man from starvation in Calcutta. He expressed his views (LSV 1.314)
19 Oct 1892 I Swamiji's mature views on life (LSV 1.315)
20 Oct 1892 S Swamiji used to furnish his discourses with scientific parellels (LSV 1.316)
21 Oct 1892 I During discussions in Belgaum Swamiji often became impatient with fanatical and obstinate people (LSV 1.316)
22 Oct 1892 I Swamiji told Haripadbabu about his intention to go to America to attend Parliament of Religions (LSV 1.317)
23 Oct 1892 I After finding Haripadbabu and his wife determined , Swamiji finally initiated them (LSV 1.317)
24 27 Oct 1892 I Though Swamiji had intention to go to Rameshwaram but he happened to stop at Margao in Goa (LSV 1.318)
