6 Aug 1886 S Before leaving the Kashipur garden-house the young monks took to Balarambabu's house, besides the Sri Ramakrishna's relics, his bedding, clothing, furniture, and the utensils which had been used in serving him. In all their subsequent moves these valuable treasures have gone with them. (LSV 1.190)

53 May 1887 S Twofold tendencies (Itinerant and Stationary monkhood) prevailed in Naren and brother disciples. (LSV 1.209)
54 May 1887 I In first week of May,Trigunatita suddenly disappeared from the monastery, saying that he was going away somewhere, but confiding his plan to no one. (LSV 1.210)
55 May 1887 CE Swamiji was agitated at sudden disappearance of Trigunatita as he was not exposed to outside world and migt meet with misfortune. He enquired with brother disciples and waited for Raja Maharaj (Brahmanand) to returmn to Math (LSV 1.210)
56 May 1887 CE Brahmananda on return handed over a note left by Trigunatita to Swamiji in which he disclosed the reason of his leaving Math. (LSV 1.210)
57 May 1887 CE Brahmanananda debated with brother disciples and Swamiji, supporting the cause of Trigunatita's leaving Math (LSV 1.211)
58 May 1887 S At later date Swamiji was also restless for pilgrimage. He conveninced brother disciples on glory of wandering life (LSV 1.211)
59 May 1887 CE Swamaji went to Antpur with Brahmananda, Premananda and Advaitananda (LSV 1.211)
60 May 1887 S In summer 1887, Swamiji had fever owing to kidney trouble (LSV 1.211)
61 May 1887 S Swamiji could make only short visits till mid 1890 (LSV 1.211)
62 May 1887 I Finally Swamiji left Varahnagar Math in July 1890 (LSV 1.212)
