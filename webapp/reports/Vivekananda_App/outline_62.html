Wild fire in the South (Jan-Feb 1897)

1 26 Jan 1897 S Swamiji and party arrived at Pamban travelling by a steamer at about 3 pm. Received by the raja of Ramnad. (LSV 2. 177)
2 Jan 1897 Lecture Swamiji's lecture REPLY TO THE ADDRESS OF WELCOME AT PAMBAN at Pamban (CW SV 3.136)
3 Jan 1897 Lecture Swamiji's lecture ON REAL WORSHIP at Rameshwaram temple (CW SV 3.141)
4 27 Jan 1897 S Swamiji visited the temple of Rameshwaram. (LSV 2. 178)
5 28 Jan 1897 S In the honor of Swamiji Raja of Ramnad fed thousands of poor people. (LSV 2. 178)
6 28 Jan 1897 S To mark the place where the Swami first set foot on Indian soil after his triumph in the West, the Raja planned to erect a victory monument, forty feet in height, bearing the following inscription: (LSV 2. 178)
7 29 Jan 1897 S Swamiji departed in the Raja's boat from Pamban at 4 am and arrived at the coast at about 6 pm. Journeyed to Ramnad in bullock cart. Grand reception in Ramnad. (LSV 2. 179)
8 29 Jan 1897 I Swamiji received in the audience hall of Ramnad. Presented address of welcome. (LSV 2. 180)
9 29 Jan 1897 Lecture Swamiji's lecture REPLY TO THE ADDRESS OF WELCOME AT RAMNAD at Ramnad (CW SV 3.144 LSV 2. 180)

14 Jan 1897 I Swamiji gave a short address on "Need of Shakti worship in India" in a phonograph. (LSV 2. 182)

25 3 Feb 1897 S Train arrived at Trichinopoly (Tiruchirapalli) On the way to Kumbakonam, at 4 am in morning. Swamiji was presented an address and he replied briefly. (LSV 2. 184)
26 3 Feb 1897 S Train arrived at Tanjore (Thanjavur) On the way to Kumbakonam. Swamiji was honored. - At Tanjore (or Thanjavur), another large demonstration had. been arranged. (LSV 2. 184)
