Calcutta: Establishment of Ramakrishna Mission Association (May 1897)

1 May 1893 I Hari Mj. Relates an incident indicative of the profound love for all men that there was in Swamiji's heart. (LSV 1.387)
2 1 May 1897 News report Indian Mirror reported return of Swami Vivekananda with his disciples. (LSV 2. 242)
3 1 May 1897 Notes Whenever the Swami came to Calcutta, he stayed for a short time at Balarambabu's house in Baghbazar. (LSV 2. 246)
4 1 May 1897 Notes Swamiji's intention to start an organization for service on all levels. (LSV 2. 246)
5 1 May 1897 ME Swamiji called a meeting of all lay and monastic disciples at 3.00 pm at Balram mandir. Here Ramakrishna Mission Association inaugurated with mother's blessings (LSV 2. 246)
6 1 May 1897 ME The proposal was enthusiastically supported by all the householder disciples. There was discussion about the future method of work. In this meeting on May I the Ramakrishna Mission Association was established. (LSV 2. 246)
7 5 May 1897 ME The second meeting at Balram mandir. Resolutions were passed lying down the main principles by which the movement was to be guided and it's aims and objectives. (LSV 2. 247)
8 5 May 1897 ME After the resolutions had been passed, office-bearers were appointed. (LSV 2. 248)
9 5 May 1897 ME When the meeting had broken up and most of the lay members had departed, the conversation between Swamiji and Swami Yogananda: (LSV 2. 249)
10 May 1897 ME Swami Adbhutananda was taking Swamiji to task for not preaching tile ideas of Shri Ramakrishna, and was challenging him to prove how his plans could be reconciled with the latter's teachings. (LSV 2. 251)
11 May 1897 I The Swami's voice became choked, his frame shook with intense emotion. (LSV 2. 252)
12 May 1897 I This incident is significant, exposing as it does the depth of the Swami's Bhakti. (LSV 2. 252)
13 May 1897 I Arguments with brother disc. Over harmony of mission work and Thakur's life and teachings. All were convinced ultimately (LSV 2. 253)

20 9 May 1897 CE The first general meeting of the members of the Ramakrishna Mission Association was held on Sunday, May 9,1897, at Balarambabu's house. (LSV 2.254)
