Puna (June 1892)

1 Jun 1892 CE Swamiji arrived in Pune alongwith Limbdi Thakore Saheb and stayed with him. This is revealed from the address of his letter to Dewan of Junagadh (LSV 1.302)
2 15 Jun 1892 Letter Swamiji's letter to DESAI HARIDAS BIHARIDAS From PUNE (CW SV 8.287)
3 Jun 1892 S Swamiji's reply to Thakore Saheb's request to come to Limdi and stay with him (LSV 1.302)
4 Jun 1892 CE Instead of Rameswaram Swamiji went to Khandava in the central Province (LSV 1.302)
